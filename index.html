Explore Colorado

The Centennial State awaits

Colorado offers something for everyone. From the vibrant streets of Denver to the majestic peaks of Rocky Mountain National Park, from world-class ski resorts to charming mountain towns, this is a state built for adventure.

Whether you're hiking fourteeners, exploring craft breweries, catching a concert at Red Rocks, or simply soaking in hot springs after a day on the slopes, Colorado delivers experiences you won't forget.

Colorado Hot Springs

Soak in natural mineral waters across the state

SPRINGS

Glenwood Springs

World's largest hot springs pool. Historic resort town with vapor caves, adventure park, and I-70 access.

SPRINGS

Pagosa Springs

World's deepest geothermal hot spring. Multiple facilities along the San Juan River with mountain views.

SPRINGS

Ouray

"Switzerland of America" with natural hot springs pool. Box canyon setting surrounded by 13,000-foot peaks.

SPRINGS

Idaho Springs

Indian Hot Springs since 1863. Cave pools, outdoor soaking, and private baths just 45 minutes from Denver.

Creekside hot springs at the base of a 14er. Natural rock pools in Chalk Creek near Buena Vista.

Strawberry Park

Rustic natural hot springs in the forest near Steamboat. Clothing-optional after dark. True Colorado experience.

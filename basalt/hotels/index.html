Basalt Hotels

Quick Info

Best for downtown: Green Drake Hotel. Best value: Vacation rentals. For fishing: Frying Pan Anglers Lodge. Budget tip: Basalt is cheaper than Aspen with RFTA bus access. Book ahead: Summer fishing season, ski weekends.

Basalt offers a more affordable alternative to staying in Aspen while keeping you in the Roaring Fork Valley. The free RFTA bus connects Basalt to Aspen in about 25 minutes, making it a practical base for skiing or exploring.

Downtown Basalt

Green Drake Hotel

Boutique hotel in downtown Basalt with fly fishing theme. Walking distance to restaurants and galleries. Modern rooms with mountain character.

Element Basalt

Extended stay hotel near downtown. Kitchen suites, pool, and modern amenities. Good for longer fishing trips or family stays.

For Anglers

Frying Pan Anglers Lodge

Lodging focused on fly fishing guests. Near the Frying Pan River with guided trips available. Book well ahead during prime fishing season.

River Cabins

Various vacation rental cabins along the Frying Pan Valley. Private river access at some properties. Check VRBO and Airbnb for options.

Vacation Rentals

Vacation rentals are popular in Basalt, offering more space and amenities than hotels. Options range from downtown condos to riverfront cabins up the Frying Pan Valley.

Nearby Options

Carbondale

10 minutes down-valley with additional lodging options. Even more affordable than Basalt.

El Jebel

Between Basalt and Carbondale with chain hotels and more budget-friendly rates.

Tips

Book for fishing season. Spring and fall are prime fly fishing times. The Frying Pan draws anglers from around the world.

Use RFTA. The free bus to Aspen means you can skip Aspen hotel prices and still access skiing and dining.

Consider Carbondale. Even more affordable, just 10 minutes away with the same bus access.

Vacation rentals work well. For groups or longer stays, rentals often offer better value than hotels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Basalt cheaper than Aspen?

Yes, significantly. Expect to pay 30-50% less for comparable lodging.

Can I get to Aspen from Basalt without a car?

Yes. RFTA buses run regularly between Basalt and Aspen. About 25 minutes.

When should I book?

1-2 months ahead for ski season and summer. Further ahead for peak fishing in spring/fall.

Are there chain hotels in Basalt?

Limited. Element Basalt is the main branded option. More chains in El Jebel and Carbondale.

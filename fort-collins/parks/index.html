Fort Collins Parks

Green spaces in the Choice City

Fort Collins maintains an excellent park system, from historic city parks to modern community spaces. With easy access to the foothills and plenty of urban green space, finding a park for any occasion is easy.

City Park

Fort Collins' Central Park

Historic park with pool, lake, playground, and picnic areas. Home to the Fort Collins City Park Nine golf course.

Lee Martinez Park

Family Favorite

Family-friendly park with farm animals, playground, fishing pond, and train rides. Perfect for kids.

Spring Canyon Community Park

Modern Community Space

Large modern park with dog park, trails, sports fields, and stunning mountain views. One of Fort Collins' best.

Large sports complex with pool, ice rink, skate park, and multiple sports fields. Year-round activities.

Beautiful park with tennis center, natural areas, and scenic pond. Great for walks and sports.

Fort Collins Dog Parks

Off-leash adventures for your best friend

Fort Collins is one of Colorado's most dog-friendly cities, with excellent off-leash options ranging from massive natural areas to neighborhood parks. Here are the best places to let your dog run free.

Spring Canyon Dog Park

Fort Collins' Best Dog Park

Large off-leash area with trails, water features, and mountain views. Part of Spring Canyon Community Park.

Large Area Water

Soft Gold Park Dog Park

Southeast Fort Collins

Popular neighborhood dog park with separate areas for large and small dogs. Well-maintained and busy.

Separate Areas Popular

Fossil Creek Dog Park

South Fort Collins

Well-designed park with large fenced area, shade structures, and benches. Part of Fossil Creek Community Park.

Fenced Shaded

Horsetooth Dog Park

At Horsetooth Reservoir

Off-leash area near Horsetooth Reservoir. Great for combining a hike with dog time in stunning scenery.

Scenic Trails

Rossborough Park Dog Park

North Fort Collins

Neighborhood dog park with good-sized fenced area. Less crowded option in north Fort Collins.

Quiet Fenced

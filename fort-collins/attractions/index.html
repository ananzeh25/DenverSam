Fort Collins Attractions

Craft beer capital meets outdoor paradise

Fort Collins combines college-town energy, a thriving craft brewery scene, and stunning access to the Rocky Mountain foothills. From the charming Old Town district to Horsetooth Reservoir's dramatic scenery, here are the essential Fort Collins experiences.

Old Town Fort Collins

Historic Downtown District

The inspiration for Disneyland's Main Street USA. Boutiques, restaurants, breweries, and tree-lined streets.

Free Shopping

Horsetooth Reservoir

Fort Collins' Backyard Lake

6.5-mile reservoir with swimming, boating, and hiking. The iconic Horsetooth Rock towers above.

Swimming Hiking

New Belgium Brewing

Craft Beer Pioneer

Home of Fat Tire. Free tours, tasting room, and riverside patio. The birthplace of Fort Collins' brewing fame.

Free Tours Beer

CSU Campus

Colorado State University

Beautiful campus with the Oval, gardens, and art galleries. Football at Canvas Stadium in fall.

Free Football

Odell Brewing Company

Award-Winning Brews

90 Shilling and beyond. Excellent taproom with food trucks, games, and one of the best patios in town.

Taproom Patio

Poudre River Trail

Riverside Pathway

21+ miles of paved trail along the Cache la Poudre River. Perfect for biking, running, and walking.

Biking 21+ mi

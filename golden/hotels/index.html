Golden Hotels

Quick Info

Best location: Downtown for walkability. Best value: Highway 6 corridor. Unique stay: The Golden Hotel. Book ahead: CSM graduation, Coors events, summer weekends. Note: Limited options; book early.

Golden has fewer hotels than Denver but offers a quieter mountain-town base. Staying here puts you walking distance to Clear Creek, downtown restaurants, and trailheads—with Denver just 15 minutes away.

Downtown Golden

The Golden Hotel

Boutique hotel right on Clear Creek. Walk to everything downtown. The Bridgewater Grill on site. Golden's best accommodation.

Table Mountain Inn

Adobe-style hotel near downtown. Restaurant and bar on site. Walking distance to Washington Avenue shops.

Highway 6 Corridor

Hampton Inn Golden

Reliable chain option near I-70. Pool and breakfast included. Good for mountain travelers.

Residence Inn Golden

Extended stay option with kitchens. Near highways for easy access to Denver or mountains.

Denver Marriott West

Full-service Marriott on the edge of Golden. Larger property with meeting space. Highway convenient.

Unique Options

Camping

Golden Gate Canyon State Park has cabins and campsites. Clear Creek RV Park for RV travelers. Seasonal availability.

Vacation Rentals

VRBO and Airbnb have options in and around Golden. Look for Clear Creek views or mountain proximity.

Tips

Book The Golden Hotel early. It's the only downtown boutique option and fills quickly.

Consider Denver alternatives. Lakewood and west Denver hotels are nearby with more selection.

Check event calendars. Colorado School of Mines events fill hotels fast.

Highway 6 for value. Chain hotels along the highway offer lower rates and easy highway access.

Mountain gateway. Golden is well-positioned for I-70 access if you're heading to ski resorts.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best hotel in Golden?

The Golden Hotel for location and character. Table Mountain Inn for a close second.

Should I stay in Golden or Denver?

Golden for mountain access and small-town vibe. Denver for more dining, nightlife, and hotel options.

Are there hotels near Coors Brewery?

The Golden Hotel and Table Mountain Inn are both walking distance. Highway hotels are a short drive.

Is Golden a good base for skiing?

Yes—you're right at the mouth of I-70. Closer to ski resorts than downtown Denver.

Margaret Carpenter Pool

Quick Info

Type: Outdoor aquatic center. Features: Slides, lazy river, play areas. Season: Summer (Memorial Day to Labor Day). Location: Adjacent to Carpenter Park. Admission: Daily fees; resident discounts. Best for: Families with kids.

Margaret Carpenter Pool is Thornton's outdoor water park—a summer destination for families looking to cool off. Located next to Carpenter Park, the facility offers slides, a lazy river, and play areas for all ages.

Features

Water Slides

Multiple slides for different thrill levels. Body slides and tube slides keep kids and teens entertained.

Lazy River

Float around the lazy river—popular with all ages. Tubes typically provided.

Zero-Depth Entry

Gradual entry area for toddlers and young children. Shallow water with spray features.

Lap Lanes

Lap swimming available during designated times for fitness swimmers.

Programs

Swim Lessons

Summer swim lessons for children. Register early—popular sessions fill quickly.

Pool Parties

Birthday party packages available. Reserve in advance for summer dates.

Special Events

Movie nights and special events throughout the summer season.

Planning Your Visit

Season

Open Memorial Day through Labor Day. Hours vary throughout the season—check before visiting.

Admission

Daily admission fees. Resident and non-resident rates. Season passes available for frequent visitors.

What to Bring

Swimsuit, sunscreen, towels, and water shoes recommended. Lockers available on-site.

Tips

Arrive early. Hot summer days get crowded. Arrive when gates open for best experience.

Bring sunscreen. Colorado sun is intense. Reapply frequently throughout the day.

Check capacity. Pool may reach capacity on hot weekends. Call ahead or arrive early.

Combine with Carpenter Park. Park adjacent—bring a picnic for before or after swimming.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does admission cost?

Varies by residency. Thornton residents pay less. Check current rates on city website.

Are tubes included?

Lazy river tubes typically provided. Some slides may require tubes available on-site.

Can I bring food?

Outside food policies vary. Concessions available on-site. Check current rules.

Is there shade?

Some shaded areas and umbrellas. Arrives early for best spots. Bring your own shade if allowed.

Good for toddlers?

Yes—zero-depth entry and spray features perfect for little ones.

Larkridge Shopping Center

Quick Info

Type: Outdoor retail center. Anchor: Target. Features: Shops, restaurants, entertainment. Location: I-25 and 136th Avenue. Parking: Free surface parking. Hours: Vary by store.

Larkridge is north Thornton's primary shopping destination—an outdoor lifestyle center at I-25 and 136th Avenue. The center serves northern metro residents with retail, dining, and entertainment.

Shopping

Anchor Stores

Target serves as the main anchor, drawing shoppers for everyday essentials and more. Additional national retailers round out the shopping options.

Specialty Retail

Mix of national chains and local retailers. Clothing, home goods, sporting goods, and specialty shops.

Services

Banks, salons, fitness studios, and professional services located throughout the center.

Dining

Sit-Down Restaurants

Full-service restaurants including casual dining chains and local options. Good variety for families and groups.

Fast Casual

Quick-service restaurants for lunch, coffee, and on-the-go meals.

Coffee & Treats

Coffee shops and dessert spots for breaks between shopping.

Entertainment

Movie Theater

Multi-screen cinema at the center. First-run films and stadium seating.

Family Activities

Family-friendly entertainment options beyond the theater.

Location & Access

By Car

Direct access from I-25 at 136th Avenue exit. Easy freeway access from anywhere in the north metro.

Parking

Ample free surface parking throughout the center. Holiday periods get busy—arrive early.

Tips

Weekday shopping. Less crowded during weekday mornings and early afternoons.

Combine trips. One-stop shopping with retail, dining, and entertainment together.

Check hours. Individual store hours vary. Confirm before traveling for specific shops.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a mall in Thornton?

Larkridge is an outdoor lifestyle center, not an enclosed mall. For traditional malls, Westminster Promenade or Flatiron Crossing are nearby.

What stores are at Larkridge?

Target is the anchor. Mix of national retailers, restaurants, and service businesses.

Is there a movie theater?

Yes—multi-screen cinema showing current releases.

How do I get there from Denver?

I-25 north to 136th Avenue exit. About 20-25 minutes from downtown depending on traffic.

Is parking free?

Yes—free surface parking throughout the center.

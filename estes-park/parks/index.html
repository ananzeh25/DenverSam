Estes Park Parks

From national park to town green spaces

Estes Park is surrounded by stunning natural spaces, from the world-famous Rocky Mountain National Park to charming town parks. Here are the essential parks in and around Estes Park.

Rocky Mountain National Park

America's Alpine Wonderland

415 square miles of peaks, lakes, and wildlife. Over 350 miles of trails, scenic drives, and endless adventure.

National Park Timed Entry

Bond Park

Downtown Riverside

Downtown park along the Big Thompson River. Performance pavilion, playground, and riverwalk access.

Events River

Lake Estes Marina & Park

Recreation Hub

Lake recreation with kayak, paddleboard, and boat rentals. Paved 3.75-mile trail around the lake.

Boating Trail

Stanley Park

Events & Recreation

Large park near the Stanley Hotel with sports fields, fairgrounds, and event space for festivals.

Events Sports

Roosevelt National Forest

Surrounding Wilderness

National forest lands surrounding Estes Park. Hiking, camping, and fishing in the Arapaho and Roosevelt forests.

Forest Camping

Estes Valley Recreation Center

Community Recreation

Public recreation center with pool, fitness center, climbing wall, and outdoor sports facilities.

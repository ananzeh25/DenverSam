Antlers Rooftop Tavern: Menu, Mountain Views &amp; What to Order in Estes Park

Antlers Rooftop Tavern sits on the third floor above downtown Estes Park, delivering panoramic Rocky Mountain views alongside elevated pub fare. The rooftop patio packs craft cocktails, Colorado game meats, and local brews with scenery that changes dramatically from snow-capped winter peaks to summer's green mountains. It's the spot where locals and tourists gather after hiking Rocky Mountain National Park.

What to Order Based on Your Taste

First-timers: Bison smash burger gives you Colorado game without being too adventurous. Add a house margarita for the full experience. Both are consistently good and represent what Antlers does best.

Adventurous eaters: Go for Rocky Mountain oysters as appetizer, then rattlesnake sausage dip. Follow with elk chili. Order the Lone Ranger cocktail. You'll have stories for home.

Playing it safe: Harvest flatbread works as light meal or shared appetizer. House salads are fresh and substantial. Regular beef burger available if game meat doesn't appeal. Beer selection is excellent.

Budget-conscious: Visit for drinks and appetizers during happy hour (3-6pm weekdays). Split the flatbread and rattlesnake dip between two people. Skip entrees, enjoy sunset, save $30 per person.

Families with kids: Kids menu offers standard fare (chicken strips, burger, mac & cheese) for $8-12. High chairs available. Rooftop railing is secure. Dogs and kids both welcome creates relaxed family vibe.

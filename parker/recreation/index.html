Parker Recreation

Quick Info

District: South Suburban Parks and Recreation. Facilities: Rec centers, pools, sports fields, trails. Programs: Youth and adult sports, fitness, aquatics, camps. Access: Residents get member rates; non-residents pay more.

Parker offers extensive recreation through the South Suburban Parks and Recreation District. The system includes recreation centers, outdoor pools, sports complexes, and trails, plus programming for all ages. Families move to Parker partly for these amenities.

Parks

Salisbury Park: The main community park with sports fields, playground, splash pad, and trail connections. Hosts community events and sports leagues.

Bar CCC Park: Large park with baseball fields, soccer fields, and open space. Connected to the Cherry Creek Trail.

Neighborhood parks: Smaller parks throughout Parker neighborhoods with playgrounds and picnic areas.

Recreation Centers

The Parker area is served by South Suburban facilities:

  • Indoor pools and aquatic facilities
  • Fitness centers with cardio and weights
  • Gymnasiums for basketball and volleyball
  • Group fitness classes
  • Youth and senior programming

Membership options range from day passes to annual memberships.

Aquatics

Swimming options include:

  • Indoor pools for year-round swimming
  • Outdoor pools open in summer
  • Swim lessons for all ages
  • Lap swimming and water fitness
  • Splash pads for young children

Youth Sports

Parker has extensive youth sports programming:

  • Soccer, baseball, softball leagues
  • Basketball and volleyball
  • Lacrosse and flag football
  • Tennis and golf programs
  • Competitive and recreational levels

Trails

The trail system connects parks and neighborhoods:

  • Cherry Creek Trail runs through Parker
  • Local connector trails link neighborhoods
  • Paved paths for biking, walking, running
  • Open space trails for nature walks

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a Parker resident?

No, but residents pay lower rates. Non-residents can use facilities and programs at higher fees.

What's the best rec center nearby?

Depends on your location and needs. South Suburban operates multiple facilities. Check their website for locations and amenities.

How do I sign up for sports leagues?

Through South Suburban's website or in person at rec centers. Registration opens seasonally—popular programs fill fast.

Are there senior programs?

Yes. South Suburban offers fitness, social, and educational programs for seniors at various locations.

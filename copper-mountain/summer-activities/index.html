Summer at Copper Mountain

Quick Info

Season: June-September. Key activities: Mountain biking, hiking, Woodward, golf. Free concerts: Weekend events in Center Village. Weather: Warm days, afternoon thunderstorms common. Lodging: Lower rates than winter.

Copper Mountain comes alive in summer with lift-served biking, hiking, free concerts, and festivals. The mountain offers a full resort experience at a fraction of ski season prices.

Mountain Biking

Lift-served downhill trails and cross-country riding. American Eagle lift hauls bikes for gravity-fed descents. Trails range from beginner flow to expert technical. See our full biking guide.

Hiking

Mountain trails accessible from the base area and via lift. Options range from easy nature walks to challenging alpine routes. See our hiking guide.

Woodward Copper

Year-round action sports training facility with trampolines, foam pits, skateparks, and more. Great for rainy days or learning new skills. See our Woodward guide.

Summer Events

Free Concerts

Weekend concert series in Center Village. Local and regional acts perform for free. Bring a chair and enjoy the music.

Festivals

Various festivals throughout summer including BBQ competitions, fitness events, and cultural celebrations.

Fourth of July

Major celebration with fireworks over the mountain. One of Summit County's best Independence Day events.

More Activities

Golf: Copper Creek Golf Course, designed by Pete and Perry Dye. Challenging mountain course at elevation.

Tennis: Resort courts available for guests.

Fishing: Nearby streams and lakes for trout fishing.

Scenic Lift Rides: Take the American Eagle lift for views without the hike.

Tips

Plan for storms. July and August bring afternoon thunderstorms most days. Morning activities are safest.

Great value. Summer lodging is much cheaper than winter. Good time for a budget mountain trip.

Check the events calendar. Free concerts and festivals add entertainment value.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Copper Mountain busy in summer?

Less crowded than winter. Weekends have more activity, weekdays are quiet.

What's open in summer?

Most restaurants, lodging, and activities operate June through September. Some venues have limited hours.

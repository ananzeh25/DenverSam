Denver PrideFest

Celebrating Love & Community

One of America's Largest Pride Celebrations

A weekend of celebration and activism

Denver PrideFest is one of the largest Pride celebrations in the country, drawing over 500,000 people to downtown Denver each June. The weekend combines a massive street festival with political advocacy, community building, and joyful celebration.

The iconic Colfax Avenue Pride Parade kicks off Sunday morning, featuring colorful floats, marching bands, community organizations, and corporate sponsors winding through Capitol Hill. The energy is electric as crowds line the streets cheering, waving flags, and celebrating visibility.

The main festival takes place in Civic Center Park, featuring live music on multiple stages, drag performances, food vendors, exhibitors, and community resources. The event celebrates LGBTQ+ pride while also raising awareness for ongoing civil rights issues.

Festival Highlights

PARADE

Pride Parade

Sunday morning down Colfax Avenue through Capitol Hill. Floats, marchers, and celebration. Arrive early for good viewing spots.

STAGE

Live Entertainment

Multiple stages with music, drag performances, DJs, and special guests. Main stage features national acts and local performers.

FEST

Festival Grounds

Civic Center Park hosts vendors, food, exhibitors, and community organizations. Free admission to main festival area.

PARTY

Related Events

Parties, brunches, and events throughout Denver the entire week. Check local listings for the full Pride Week schedule.

Crested Butte Wildflowers

The Wildflower Capital of Colorado

Nature's Greatest Display

When meadows explode with color

Crested Butte earned its title as the "Wildflower Capital of Colorado" honestly. Each July, the meadows surrounding this remote mountain town explode with lupine, columbine, Indian paintbrush, and dozens of other species in one of North America's most spectacular floral displays.

The Crested Butte Wildflower Festival celebrates this natural phenomenon with a week of guided hikes, photography workshops, botanical walks, art classes, and educational programs. Experts from across the country lead participants into the backcountry to explore the diversity of high-altitude flora.

Peak bloom typically occurs mid-July, though timing varies with snowpack and weather. The festival is scheduled to coincide with the most reliable bloom period. Beyond the official events, the surrounding wilderness offers endless opportunities for self-guided exploration through carpets of color.

Festival Highlights

HIKE

Guided Hikes

Botanists and naturalists lead groups into prime wildflower areas. Learn identification, ecology, and medicinal uses. All skill levels welcome.

PHOTO

Photography Workshops

Professional photographers teach macro and landscape techniques. Sunrise and sunset sessions. Capture the color at its most spectacular.

ART

Art Classes

Plein air painting, botanical illustration, and nature journaling. Local artists guide participants in capturing flowers on paper.

GARDEN

Garden Tours

Visit private gardens designed for high-altitude growing. Learn techniques for cultivating native species. Inspiration for home gardeners.

Best Wildflower Spots

WASH

Washington Gulch

Classic Crested Butte wildflower hike. Meadows of lupine and sunflowers with Gothic Mountain backdrop. Moderate trail accessible to most.

GOTHIC

Gothic Road

Drive to the old mining town of Gothic. Roadside fields of color. Rocky Mountain Biological Laboratory research station.

SNODGRASS

Snodgrass Trail

Accessible trail with stunning views. Wildflower-filled meadows below Mount Crested Butte. Good for families and photographers.

KEBLER

Kebler Pass

Famous for fall colors but beautiful in summer too. Wildflower meadows along the road. Largest aspen grove in Colorado.

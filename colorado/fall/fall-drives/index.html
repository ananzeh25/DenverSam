Scenic Fall Drives

Roads lined with gold

Leaf Peeping by Car

Colorado's most spectacular autumn routes

Colorado's mountain passes and byways become golden corridors each fall as millions of aspen trees change color. Scenic drives offer the most accessible way to experience the spectacle - no hiking required, just stunning views from your car window.

Peak color typically occurs late September to early October, moving from higher elevations down. The best drives pass through aspen groves between 8,000 and 11,000 feet. Mix in evergreen contrast, dramatic mountain peaks, and Colorado's famous blue skies for unforgettable scenery.

Popular routes get crowded on fall weekends. Start early - trailheads and pullouts fill by 8 AM on peak color Saturdays. Weekdays are significantly less crowded. Many passes close for winter in mid to late October, so time your visit carefully.

Top Fall Drives

KEBLER

Kebler Pass

Largest aspen grove in Colorado. Dirt road from Crested Butte to Paonia. 30 miles of gold. Passenger car passable. Peak late September.

INDEP

Independence Pass

Highest paved crossing in Colorado (12,095'). Connects Aspen to Leadville. Stunning at treeline. Closes mid-October for winter.

MAROON

Maroon Bells Road

Most photographed peaks in Colorado. Shuttle required during peak times. Aspens with iconic mountain backdrop. Arrive before sunrise for parking.

PEAK

Peak to Peak Highway

55 miles from Estes Park to Black Hawk. Closest great fall color to Denver. Paved and open year-round. Good weekday alternative.

More Great Routes

GUANELLA

Guanella Pass

Close to Denver (1.5 hours). Paved road Georgetown to Grant. Stunning aspens, mountain views. Peak early October. Popular but accessible.

DALLAS

Dallas Divide

Between Ridgway and Telluride. Sneffels Range backdrop. Perhaps the most dramatic mountain/aspen combination anywhere. Classic Colorado scene.

MILLION

Million Dollar Highway

Silverton to Ouray. Dramatic cliffs and mountain passes. Fall color plus stunning engineering. Part of the San Juan Skyway.

TRAIL

Trail Ridge Road

Rocky Mountain National Park. Color peaks below treeline in early October. Highest continuous paved road in US. Closes late October.

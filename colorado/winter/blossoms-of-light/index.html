Blossoms of Light

Denver Botanic Gardens

One Million Lights

Denver's beloved holiday tradition

Every holiday season, Denver Botanic Gardens transforms into a glowing wonderland for Blossoms of Light. Over one million lights illuminate the gardens' paths, trees, and structures, creating an enchanting evening stroll through winter beauty.

The display includes synchronized light shows, themed garden areas, and stunning illuminated sculptures. Warm up with hot cocoa or mulled wine at the refreshment stations. Live music and carolers add to the festive atmosphere on select nights.

The event runs from mid-November through early January, including special holiday nights and New Year's Eve celebrations. It's become one of Denver's most cherished winter traditions, drawing hundreds of thousands of visitors each season.

Visiting Tips

TICKET

Tickets

Timed-entry tickets required. Weekend slots sell out quickly - book 2-3 weeks ahead. Member presale offers early access.

TIME

Best Time

Weeknights are less crowded. Later time slots (after 7 PM) tend to thin out. Allow 1-2 hours to explore.

WARM

Staying Warm

Dress warmly - you're outside for an hour or more. Heated tents offer breaks. Hot drinks available throughout.

PHOTO

Photography

Tripods not allowed on busy nights. Many Instagram-worthy spots. Best photos come after full darkness.

Glenwood Hot Springs

World's largest hot springs pool

A Colorado Icon

Over 125 years of soaking history

Glenwood Hot Springs Pool has been drawing visitors since 1888, and it remains Colorado's most famous hot springs destination. The main pool stretches over two blocks - more than a football field in length - making it the world's largest hot springs pool. Over 3.5 million gallons of naturally heated mineral water fill the pools daily.

The resort sits in the heart of downtown Glenwood Springs, an easy 2.5-hour drive from Denver on I-70. The location makes it perfect for a day trip or as part of a longer mountain vacation. Many visitors combine a soak with skiing at nearby Sunlight or Aspen resorts.

Winter is magical here. Steam rises from the enormous pool against a backdrop of snow-covered cliffs. The water stays at a comfortable 90-93°F in the big pool and 104°F in the therapy pool. You can swim laps, float lazily, or simply sit in the hot water watching snowflakes melt on contact.

What to Know

POOL

Two Pools

Main pool is 405 feet long at 90-93°F - warm enough to enjoy, cool enough to swim. Therapy pool at 104°F for serious soaking.

SLIDES

Water Slides

Two water slides add fun for families. Additional fee. The plunge into warm water at the bottom is a thrill in winter.

TIME

Hours & Prices

Open daily year-round. Adults around $30, kids less. Evening rates available. Holiday periods busy - arrive early.

STAY

Lodge on Site

Glenwood Hot Springs Lodge offers poolside rooms. Unlimited pool access included. Walk from your room to the water in minutes.

Tips for Visiting

CROWD

Beat the Crowds

Weekday mornings are quietest. Weekend afternoons most crowded. Winter holidays pack the pool. Evening soaks are atmospheric.

BRING

What to Bring

Swimsuit required. Towels and lockers available for rent. Bring flip-flops for the deck. Waterproof phone case for photos.

FOOD

Dining Options

On-site grill serves food poolside. Downtown Glenwood Springs walkable with many restaurants. Don't swim on an empty stomach.

COMBO

Combine with Skiing

Sunlight Mountain is 10 minutes away. Aspen 45 minutes. Perfect après-ski destination after a day on the slopes.

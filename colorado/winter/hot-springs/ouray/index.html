Ouray Hot Springs

Mountain peaks in every direction

The Switzerland of America

Soaking with 13,000-foot peaks all around

Ouray Hot Springs Pool offers one of Colorado's most dramatic hot springs settings. The town of Ouray sits in a box canyon surrounded by towering 13,000-foot San Juan peaks. From the pool, you're surrounded by mountains on all sides - a natural amphitheater of rock and snow.

Unlike many Colorado hot springs, Ouray's water is sulfur-free, meaning no rotten-egg smell. The naturally filtered mineral water comes from several springs and is odorless and clear. It's a welcome difference for visitors sensitive to the sulfur common at other locations.

The municipally-owned pool has served visitors since 1927. Recent renovations added new pools while maintaining the historic character. It's a community gathering spot for locals and a bucket-list destination for visitors - affordable, accessible, and absolutely stunning.

Pool Details

POOLS

Multiple Sections

Several pools at different temperatures from 96-106°F. Lap swimming area. Activity pool with climbing wall. Something for everyone.

ADULT

Adults-Only Area

Infinity-edge hot pool for ages 18+. Quieter atmosphere for relaxation. The hottest water at 106°F. Best mountain views.

SULFUR

No Sulfur Smell

Naturally sulfur-free water. No "hot springs smell." Clear, clean-feeling mineral water. Won't linger on skin or swimsuit.

VALUE

Great Value

Municipal pool keeps prices reasonable. Adults around $20. Less expensive than resort hot springs. Community facility feel.

Visiting Ouray

ICE

Ice Climbing Capital

Ouray is America's ice climbing capital. Ice Park offers beginner to expert routes. Hot springs après-climb is tradition.

DRIVE

Million Dollar Highway

Scenic US-550 runs through town. Part of the San Juan Skyway. Dramatic driving but can be snowy in winter.

TOWN

Historic Downtown

Victorian mining-era architecture. Walkable main street. Restaurants, shops, and galleries. Genuine mountain town atmosphere.

STAY

Small Town Lodging

Historic hotels and B&Bs. Limited rooms - book ahead. Wiesbaden Hot Springs offers private vapor caves. Intimate scale.

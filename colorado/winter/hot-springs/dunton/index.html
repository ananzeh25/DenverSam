Dunton Hot Springs

Ghost town turned luxury retreat

Colorado's Most Exclusive

A restored mining town in the wilderness

Dunton Hot Springs is unlike any other hot springs in Colorado - or anywhere else. This restored 1800s ghost town sits at 9,000 feet in a remote valley of the San Juan Mountains, accessible only by dirt road. Hand-hewn log cabins have been meticulously restored and outfitted with world-class luxury. Natural hot springs flow through the property.

The experience is all-inclusive and exclusive. The entire property accommodates only about 40 guests at a time. Rates start around $2,000 per night, and the waiting list for winter dates can stretch months. This isn't a casual hot springs stop - it's a destination resort for those seeking the ultimate Colorado experience.

For those who can access it, Dunton delivers something extraordinary. Soaking in a natural hot spring inside a restored bathhouse while snow falls outside, then warming up by the fire in a cabin that Butch Cassidy once visited - it's a uniquely Colorado fantasy made real.

No cell service. No Wi-Fi in cabins. Deliberate disconnection. Focus on nature and presence. Digital detox included.

Winter Activities

HELI

Heli-Skiing

Access to pristine backcountry terrain. San Juan powder runs. Expert guides. The ultimate ski experience combined with hot springs.

NORDIC

Cross-Country

Miles of groomed Nordic trails. Equipment provided. Explore the valley on skis. Return to hot springs for recovery.

SHOE

Snowshoeing

Guided snowshoe adventures available. Explore surrounding wilderness. Wildlife viewing opportunities. Peaceful winter immersion.

SPA

Wellness

Yoga sessions available. Massage and bodywork. Focus on restoration. Combine with hot springs for complete renewal.

Planning Your Visit

BOOK

Reservations

Book well in advance - months for peak season. Minimum stays may apply. Not available for day visits. Overnight guests only.

ACCESS

Getting There

About 1 hour from Telluride via dirt road. 4WD recommended. Transfer service available. Remote by design.

COST

Investment

Rates from $2,000+/night. All-inclusive. Special occasions, honeymoons, milestone celebrations. Splurge-worthy for once-in-a-lifetime.

ALT

Alternatives

If budget doesn't allow, other hot springs deliver great experiences for less. No shame in Glenwood or Ouray. Dunton is aspirational.

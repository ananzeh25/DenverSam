Strawberry Park

Rustic and real

Colorado's Most Authentic

Natural pools in the wilderness

Strawberry Park Hot Springs offers something different from Colorado's resort-style hot springs. Here, natural rock pools are built into the hillside along Hot Springs Creek. The setting is forested and wild. Facilities are rustic. The vibe is decidedly earthy and countercultural.

Located about 7 miles north of Steamboat Springs, Strawberry Park has been operating since 1982. The springs bubble up at 147°F and are cooled by creek water to soaking temperatures ranging from 101-106°F depending on which pool you choose and how much cold water is flowing.

The experience is clothing-optional after dark, which has cemented its reputation as a free-spirited alternative to more commercial springs. Daytime visits are family-friendly with suits required. Either way, you're soaking in a genuine mountain setting that feels worlds away from resort pools.

What to Know

POOLS

Natural Stone Pools

Multiple pools at different temps. Hot water flows from source, mixes with cold creek water. Find your perfect temperature by moving around.

NIGHT

Clothing-Optional

Swimsuits required during day. After dark, clothing is optional. Adults only (18+) after dark. Know before you go.

4WD

4WD Required

Winter road is steep and unpaved. 4WD or chains mandatory. Shuttle service available from Steamboat. Don't risk it in 2WD.

CASH

Cash Only

No credit cards accepted. Bring cash for admission. ATM in Steamboat but not on-site. Plan ahead.

Planning Your Visit

RESERVE

Reservations

Reservations required, especially weekends. Book online in advance. Limited capacity keeps it from feeling crowded.

SHUTTLE

Shuttle Service

If you don't have 4WD, use the shuttle from Steamboat. Runs regularly during operating hours. Easier than white-knuckling the road.

CABIN

Overnight Stays

Rustic cabins and covered wagons available. Wake up and soak. Very limited availability - book months ahead for winter.

SKI

After Skiing

Perfect après-ski from Steamboat Resort. Night soaking under stars after a ski day is magical. Plan for the shuttle.

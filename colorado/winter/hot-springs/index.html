Colorado Hot Springs

Winter's ultimate reward

Soak in the Snow

Nothing beats steaming mineral waters surrounded by winter

There's a particular magic to soaking in 104-degree mineral water while snowflakes fall around you and steam rises into the cold mountain air. Colorado's geothermal springs have drawn visitors for centuries, and winter is the most spectacular time to experience them.

The state has over 30 developed hot springs, from massive resort pools to hidden wilderness soaks. Water temperatures range from warm (95°F) to nearly too-hot-to-handle (112°F). Each has its own character - some are party scenes, others feel like spiritual retreats.

After a day of skiing, hiking, or exploring, sliding into hot mineral water is the perfect end. Many visitors plan entire trips around hot springs, combining soaks with winter activities for the ultimate Colorado experience.

Top Winter Hot Springs

GLEN

Glenwood Hot Springs

World's largest hot springs pool. Two pools at different temperatures. Open year-round, magical in snow. Downtown Glenwood Springs location.

PAGOSA

Pagosa Hot Springs

World's deepest geothermal hot spring. Multiple pools along the San Juan River. Springs Resort offers 23 soaking pools at varying temps.

OURAY

Ouray Hot Springs

Historic public pool surrounded by dramatic mountain peaks. Sulfur-free waters. Multiple pools including adults-only section. Open year-round.

STRAW

Strawberry Park

Rustic natural pools near Steamboat Springs. Clothing-optional after dark. Requires 4WD in winter. Most authentic wilderness experience.

More Great Soaks

IRON

Iron Mountain

Glenwood Springs' newer option. 16 pools at various temperatures. More intimate than the giant pool. Rooftop hot tubs with canyon views.

MT PRINCE

Mt. Princeton

Chalk Creek runs through multiple pools near Buena Vista. Creek-side soaking. Historic resort with modern amenities. Stunning mountain setting.

INDIAN

Indian Hot Springs

Idaho Springs - closest hot springs to Denver (45 min). Historic caves and outdoor pools. Perfect après-ski stop from the ski resorts.

DUNTON

Dunton Hot Springs

Exclusive ghost-town resort near Telluride. Natural source pools. Luxury experience in restored 1800s mining camp. Reservations required.

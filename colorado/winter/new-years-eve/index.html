New Year's Eve in Colorado

Ring in the new year mountain-style

Celebrate in the Rockies

Fireworks, parties, and fresh powder

New Year's Eve in Colorado offers something for everyone - from massive fireworks over mountain towns to sophisticated parties in Denver, or quiet celebrations in hot springs under starry skies. The state transforms into a winter celebration destination.

Mountain towns go all out. Breckenridge drops a 20-foot illuminated star from the top of Main Street while fireworks explode overhead. Vail hosts torch-lit ski parades down the mountain followed by fireworks in Vail Village. Aspen throws legendary parties that attract celebrities from around the world.

Denver celebrates with fireworks on 16th Street Mall and events at bars and restaurants throughout the city. For something different, soak in hot springs at midnight in Glenwood or Pagosa Springs, watching fireworks reflected in steaming water.

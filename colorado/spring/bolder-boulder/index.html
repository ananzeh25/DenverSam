Bolder Boulder

America's All-American 10K

More Than a Race

A Memorial Day tradition since 1979

The Bolder Boulder isn't just a race - it's a 6.2-mile party through the streets of Boulder. Every Memorial Day, over 50,000 runners, joggers, and walkers traverse a course lined with live bands, belly dancers, Elvis impersonators, and enthusiastic spectators offering everything from beer to bacon.

The race finishes inside Folsom Field, CU Boulder's football stadium, where runners cross the line to cheering crowds. A Memorial Day tribute honors fallen service members, and elite international runners compete for substantial prize money in the professional race that follows the citizen waves.

Costumes are encouraged and creativity abounds. You'll see running bananas, superheroes, and elaborate group themes. Speed doesn't matter for most participants - the experience of running through Boulder's neighborhoods with the Flatirons as backdrop is the point.

Race Day Experience

COURSE

The Course

6.2 miles through Boulder neighborhoods. Rolling hills, beautiful scenery, and entertainment at every turn. Finish inside Folsom Field.

PARTY

On-Course Fun

30+ bands, belly dancers, slip-n-slides, costume contests, and neighbors handing out treats. The party never stops.

REG

Registration

Opens in January. Popular waves sell out fast. Costumes and casual runners welcome - no time requirements.

TRIBUTE

Memorial Tribute

Moving Memorial Day ceremony in Folsom Field honoring fallen service members. Patriotic and powerful tradition.

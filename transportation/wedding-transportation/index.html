Colorado Wedding Transportation

Elegant Arrivals for Your Mountain Wedding

Wedding Transportation Planning

Getting everyone there beautifully and on time

Colorado mountain weddings are magical—but they come with logistical challenges. Remote venues, mountain roads, altitude, and weather all require careful transportation planning.

Whether you need a romantic getaway car for the newlyweds, shuttles for guests between hotel and venue, or full wedding weekend transportation, here's what to know.

Typical Wedding Transportation Costs

Service Typical Cost Duration

Wedding Transportation Types

Options for every part of your day

Bridal Party Transport

Getting the wedding party to the venue together—time for photos, champagne, and avoiding dress disasters.

  • Stretch limo: Classic choice, fits 8-10 with gowns
  • Luxury SUV stretch: More headroom for updos
  • Party bus: Standing room, music, bar service
  • Executive Sprinter: Elegant, spacious, modern

Getaway Car

Your exit as newlyweds—iconic photos and a romantic departure.

  • Classic car: Vintage Rolls-Royce, Bentley
  • Modern luxury: Mercedes S-Class, Tesla
  • Convertible: Summer weddings, weather permitting
  • Specialty: Horse-drawn carriage, vintage truck

Guest Shuttles

Essential for mountain venues—keep guests safe and on time.

  • Charter buses: 25-55 passengers
  • Mini coaches: 15-24 passengers
  • Multiple runs: Hotel to venue and back
  • Late night service: Safe rides after celebration

VIP Family Transport

Parents, grandparents, and special guests deserve special care.

  • Luxury sedans: Comfortable for elderly guests
  • SUVs: Easier entry/exit
  • Dedicated drivers: Door-to-door service

Popular Colorado Wedding Venues

Transportation considerations by location

Estes Park / Rocky Mountain

  • 70 miles from Denver, 1.5 hours
  • Many venues have limited parking—shuttles essential
  • Trail Ridge Road closures in winter
  • The Stanley Hotel, Della Terra, Chapel on the Rock

Vail / Beaver Creek

  • 100 miles from Denver, 2+ hours
  • Consider flying guests into Eagle County Airport
  • Luxury venues expect matching transportation
  • Four Seasons, Arrabelle, Sonnenalp

Aspen

  • 200 miles from Denver, 4+ hours
  • Many guests fly into Aspen airport
  • Premium pricing for everything
  • St. Regis, Hotel Jerome, Maroon Bells

Colorado Springs / Garden of the Gods

  • 70 miles from Denver, 1 hour
  • Easier logistics than mountain venues
  • Garden of the Gods Club, Cheyenne Mountain, Broadmoor

Boulder

  • 45 miles from Denver, 45 minutes
  • Chautauqua, St. Julien, Flagstaff Mountain
  • Relatively easy guest transport from Denver

Wedding Transport Checklist

Planning your wedding day logistics

6+ Months Before

  • Research transportation companies
  • Get quotes for all needed services
  • Book preferred vehicles (popular dates go fast)
  • Coordinate with wedding planner

3 Months Before

  • Confirm all bookings in writing
  • Finalize pickup times and locations
  • Communicate shuttle info to guests
  • Plan parking for guests who drive

1 Month Before

  • Final walkthrough with transportation company
  • Confirm driver names and phone numbers
  • Prepare timeline for drivers
  • Arrange decorations for getaway car

Week Of

  • Confirm all details one final time
  • Share driver contacts with wedding party
  • Check weather forecasts for backup plans
  • Prepare gratuity envelopes for drivers

Frequently Asked Questions

Wedding transportation

How far in advance should I book wedding transportation?

6-12 months for peak wedding season (June-October). Premium vehicles and Saturday dates book earliest. Holiday weekends may require even earlier booking.

Do we need guest shuttles for a mountain wedding?

Usually yes. Mountain venues often have limited parking, winding access roads, and liability concerns. Shuttles keep guests safe (especially after drinking) and ensure everyone arrives on time.

Is gratuity included in wedding transportation?

Sometimes for wedding packages, often not for hourly bookings. Confirm in writing. Standard gratuity is 18-20% for excellent service. Many couples prepare cash envelopes in advance.

What about winter mountain weddings?

Book companies experienced with winter mountain driving. Confirm vehicles have AWD and proper tires. Build extra time into schedules for weather. Have contingency plans for road closures.

Can we decorate the getaway car?

Usually yes, with restrictions. Most allow window paint, "Just Married" signs, ribbons, and cans. Usually no tape on paint, nothing that might damage the vehicle. Confirm policies when booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Corporate transportation

Can we set up a corporate account for regular use?

Yes, most transportation companies offer corporate accounts with simplified booking, consolidated billing, and volume discounts. Typically requires a minimum monthly commitment.

How far in advance should we book for a conference?

2-4 weeks for standard service, 6-8 weeks for large events or peak seasons. Major conferences may have transportation sponsors—check before independently booking.

Can vehicles be branded with our company logo?

Many providers offer temporary branding for events—magnetic signs, window clings, or full wraps for longer engagements. Discuss options and lead time when booking.

What about last-minute changes to the schedule?

Professional services accommodate reasonable changes. Communicate with your dispatcher. Major changes (additional vehicles, different destinations) may incur fees depending on availability.

Is Wi-Fi available in corporate vehicles?

Most executive sedans, SUVs, and Sprinters offer Wi-Fi. Charter buses vary—confirm when booking if connectivity is important for your team.

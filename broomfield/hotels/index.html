Where to Stay in Broomfield

Quick Info

Top area: Interlocken business district. Luxury option: Omni Interlocken Resort. Budget options: Chain hotels along US-36. Location advantage: Midway between Denver and Boulder. Book ahead: During events at 1stBank Center.

Broomfield offers solid hotel options, anchored by the Omni Interlocken Resort. The central location between Denver and Boulder makes it a practical base for exploring both cities.

Most hotels cluster in the Interlocken area or near Flatiron Crossing, both with easy US-36 access.

Interlocken Area

Omni Interlocken Resort

The premier option in Broomfield. Full-service resort with golf course, spa, pools, and dining. Business and leisure amenities. Views of the Flatirons from many rooms.

Best for: Business travelers, golfers, those wanting resort amenities.

Aloft Broomfield Denver

Modern, tech-forward hotel in Interlocken. Good for business travelers wanting contemporary style without full resort pricing.

Other Interlocken Options

Several business-oriented hotels in the Interlocken campus. Good for corporate travel with meeting facilities nearby.

Flatiron Crossing Area

Chain Hotels

Multiple national chains near Flatiron Crossing mall. Hampton Inn, Residence Inn, and similar options. Good for shopping trips or events at 1stBank Center.

Best for: Budget-conscious travelers, families, those prioritizing shopping access.

Location Considerations

Denver Access

About 15 miles to downtown Denver via US-36. Express lanes make commute predictable. Good base if you want to avoid downtown prices.

Boulder Access

About 10 miles to Boulder. Quick drive to Pearl Street and CU campus. Consider Broomfield if Boulder hotels are full or expensive.

Airport Access

About 30 miles to Denver International. Allow 45-60 minutes depending on traffic.

Tips

Interlocken for business. If traveling for work in the tech corridor, Interlocken hotels are convenient.

Consider Boulder alternatives. When Boulder is expensive, Broomfield offers similar access at lower prices.

Event weekends. Major events at 1stBank Center can affect availability and rates. Book ahead.

US-36 express lanes. The toll lanes make Denver and Boulder commutes predictable. Factor into lodging decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Broomfield a good base for Denver?

It can work—15 miles to downtown with express lane access. Better for those with business in the tech corridor or wanting to explore both Denver and Boulder.

What's the best hotel in Broomfield?

Omni Interlocken is the only full-service resort. Otherwise, quality chain hotels serve various needs.

Are hotels cheaper than Boulder?

Generally yes, especially for chain hotels. Omni Interlocken is comparable to Boulder's nicer options.

Is there public transit to Denver?

RTD Flatiron Flyer buses run along US-36. Service to Union Station available but driving is more common.

Are there vacation rentals?

Some Airbnb/VRBO options exist but hotels dominate in Broomfield.

Golf in Broomfield

Quick Info

Top course: Broadlands Golf Course. Type: Public daily fee. Season: Year-round, weather permitting. Views: Front Range and Flatirons. Booking: Online tee times available.

Broomfield offers solid public golf options with Front Range views. The Broadlands Golf Course anchors local golf, while several other courses are within easy driving distance.

Colorado's golf season extends longer than most expect—mild days in late fall and early spring make golf possible beyond summer months.

Broadlands Golf Course

Overview

The primary Broomfield golf destination. Public course with 18 holes winding through the Broadlands community. Well-maintained with reasonable green fees.

Course

Links-style layout with some elevation changes. Multiple tee boxes accommodate various skill levels. Views of the Flatirons and Front Range throughout.

Facilities

Pro shop, driving range, and dining options. Cart rental available. Club rental for visitors.

Booking

Tee times available online. Book ahead for weekend mornings. Twilight rates offer savings.

Nearby Courses

Omni Interlocken

27-hole resort course at the Omni Interlocken Hotel. Higher-end option with excellent conditioning.

Indian Peaks Golf Course

Public course in Lafayette, just minutes from Broomfield. Good value option.

Flatirons Golf Course

Boulder municipal course accessible from Broomfield. Another public option with mountain views.

Golf Season

Peak Season

May through September offers the best conditions. Warm days and green courses. Book weekend tee times early.

Shoulder Season

April and October can be excellent—fewer crowds and good weather. Conditions may vary.

Winter Golf

Mild winter days allow golf when courses are open. Check conditions and dress in layers.

Tips

Book ahead for weekends. Weekend morning tee times fill up. Book online in advance.

Twilight rates. Afternoon rounds are cheaper and often less crowded.

Altitude affects play. Ball travels farther at altitude. Adjust club selection accordingly.

Hydrate. Colorado's dry air and altitude require more water than you might expect.

Layer up. Morning rounds can start cool and warm significantly. Dress in layers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best public course?

Broadlands is the primary Broomfield option. Omni Interlocken is higher-end. Indian Peaks offers good value.

Can I rent clubs?

Yes—most courses offer club rentals. Call ahead to confirm availability.

How much does a round cost?

Varies by course and time. Expect $40-80 for public courses, more for premium options.

Are courses walkable?

Some are, some aren't. Check with individual courses. Carts are available everywhere.

How does altitude affect my game?

Ball travels 10-15% farther at this altitude. Club up less than you think on approach shots.

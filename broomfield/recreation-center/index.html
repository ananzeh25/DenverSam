Paul Derda Recreation Center

Quick Info

Facilities: Pools, fitness center, gym, track. Programs: Classes, sports, camps. Location: 13201 Lowell Blvd. Access: Day passes and memberships available. Hours: Early morning to late evening; check seasonal schedule.

Paul Derda Recreation Center is Broomfield's flagship community recreation facility. The full-service center offers pools, fitness equipment, courts, and extensive programming for all ages.

For residents and visitors alike, it's a great option for staying active—especially in winter when outdoor options are limited.

Aquatics

Pools

Multiple pool areas including lap pool, leisure pool, and water features. Something for serious swimmers and families playing in the water.

Water Features

Slides and play features in the leisure pool area. Popular with kids and families.

Lap Swimming

Dedicated lap lanes for fitness swimming. Check schedule for lap swim times.

Fitness

Fitness Center

Full-service gym with cardio equipment, weight machines, and free weights. Well-maintained equipment and adequate space.

Group Fitness

Group exercise classes including yoga, spin, strength, and aerobics. Schedule varies—check online for current offerings.

Indoor Track

Walking and running track for indoor cardio. Good option during inclement weather.

Sports Facilities

Gymnasium

Basketball courts available for open gym and leagues. Check schedule for availability.

Racquet Sports

Courts available for racquetball and similar activities.

Programs

Youth Programs

Swim lessons, youth sports, camps, and after-school programs. Extensive youth programming throughout the year.

Adult Programs

Adult sports leagues, fitness classes, and specialized programs.

Senior Programs

Programs tailored for seniors including fitness classes and activities.

Access & Fees

Day Passes

Day passes available for non-residents and visitors. Good option for travelers wanting a workout or pool access.

Memberships

Monthly and annual memberships for regular users. Resident and non-resident rates available.

Tips

Check the schedule. Pool and facility schedules vary. Check online before visiting.

Peak times. After work and weekends are busiest. Early mornings and mid-day are quieter.

Bring your own gear. Towels and toiletries aren't provided. Bring what you need.

Kids programming. Registration often fills quickly for popular programs. Register early.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can non-residents use the facility?

Yes—day passes and non-resident memberships are available at higher rates than resident pricing.

Is there childcare?

Check with the facility for current childcare options and availability.

How are the pools?

Well-maintained and clean. Good mix of lap swimming and family play areas.

Is the fitness center crowded?

Can be during peak times. Early morning and mid-day are less busy.

Are there lockers?

Yes—locker rooms with changing facilities and showers.

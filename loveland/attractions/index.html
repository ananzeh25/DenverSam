Loveland Attractions

The Sweetheart City

Loveland is known for its public art, Valentine's Day traditions, and easy access to Rocky Mountain National Park. This welcoming Colorado city combines art, outdoor recreation, and small-town charm.

Sculpture in the Park

Largest Outdoor Sculpture Show

August event featuring 2,000+ sculptures from 160 artists. Benson Sculpture Garden hosts year-round art.

Art Free

Benson Sculpture Garden

Outdoor Sculpture Park

10-acre park with 160+ permanent sculptures. Free admission, beautiful grounds, and rotating exhibits.

Free Sculpture

Devil's Backbone

Natural Area

Dramatic rock formations and hiking trails. Wildlife viewing, rock climbing, and stunning scenery.

Hiking Free

Valentine Re-Mailing Program

Sweetheart City Tradition

Send Valentines through Loveland for a special postmark. Tradition since 1947, 100,000+ cards annually.

February Romance

Local Art & History

Art galleries and local history exhibits in a downtown building. Rotating exhibits and community events.

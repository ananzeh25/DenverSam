Loveland Hiking Trails

Foothills adventures at your doorstep

Loveland sits at the gateway to the Rocky Mountains, with excellent hiking ranging from dramatic rock formations to Rocky Mountain National Park access. Here are the essential Loveland hikes.

Devil's Backbone

Dramatic Rock Formations

Striking hogback formations with multiple loop options. Wildlife, rock climbing, and stunning scenery.

2-6 mi Moderate

Blue Sky Trail

Long-Distance Trail

20-mile trail connecting Loveland to Fort Collins. Great for long hikes, biking, or running sections.

20 mi Various

Rimrock Open Space

Foothills Loop

Loop trails through rolling foothills with wildflowers and wildlife. Great for sunrise or sunset hikes.

3-5 mi Easy

Bobcat Ridge Natural Area

Wildlife Haven

2,500 acres with diverse ecosystems and excellent wildlife viewing. Homesteader historic sites.

Various Wildlife

RMNP Gateway

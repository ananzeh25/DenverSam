Loveland Parks

Green spaces in the Sweetheart City

Loveland maintains an excellent park system with over 40 parks, from sculpture gardens to sports complexes. Here are the best parks for picnics, recreation, and enjoying the outdoors.

Benson Sculpture Garden

Art & Nature

10-acre sculpture park with 160+ artworks. Beautiful grounds for walking, picnics, and art appreciation.

Free Sculpture

North Lake Park

Lake Recreation

Popular park with lake, walking path, playground, and picnic areas. Hosts community events year-round.

Lake Playground

Mehaffey Park

Sports & Recreation

Large park with dog park, sports fields, playground, and pavilion. Home to many youth sports leagues.

Dog Park Sports

Foote Lagoon

Downtown Water Feature

Downtown park with lagoon, walking paths, and connection to Benson Sculpture Garden. Summer concerts.

Events Downtown

Loveland Sports Park

Athletic Complex

Major sports complex with fields for soccer, baseball, and softball. Hosts tournaments and leagues.

Sports Fields

Littleton, Colorado

Historic Downtown, Mountain Views

Your Guide to Littleton

History, gardens, and outdoor recreation

Littleton's historic downtown is one of the Denver metro's most charming. Main Street runs along the South Platte River with shops, restaurants, and a genuine small-town atmosphere. The light rail connects downtown Littleton directly to Denver.

Hudson Gardens offers 30 acres of cultivated gardens along the South Platte, with concert series and events throughout the year. Nearby Chatfield Reservoir provides water recreation and trail access.

The city balances suburban convenience with historic character. The Littleton Museum tells local history through living history farms and exhibits.

Explore Littleton

Downtown, gardens, and trails

TOWN

Historic Downtown

Main Street along the South Platte with local shops, restaurants, and events. Genuine small-town charm.

GARDEN

Hudson Gardens

30-acre botanical gardens with themed areas. Summer concert series and year-round events.

LAKE

Chatfield Reservoir

State park with swimming beach, boating, fishing, and trails. Hot air balloon launches in summer.

MUSEUM

Littleton Museum

Free museum with living history farms and local exhibits. Experience 1860s and 1890s Colorado life.

TRAIL

Mary Carter Greenway

Trail along the South Platte River connecting parks and neighborhoods. Part of the regional system.

TRAIN

Light Rail Access

Downtown Littleton station on the C and D lines. Direct service to downtown Denver.

Frequently Asked Questions

Planning your Littleton visit

How far is Littleton from Denver?

About 10 miles south of downtown Denver. Light rail takes about 25 minutes; driving varies with traffic.

Is downtown Littleton walkable?

Yes, very. Main Street is compact with shops and restaurants in a few-block area. The light rail station is right downtown.

Are Hudson Gardens worth visiting?

Yes, especially in bloom seasons. The summer concert series is popular. Check the schedule for special events.

What's at Chatfield?

Reservoir with swimming beach, boating, and fishing. Also hiking and biking trails, hot air balloons, and a botanic gardens satellite.

Littleton Light Rail

Quick Info

Lines: C and D lines. Key station: Downtown Littleton. To Denver: About 25 minutes to Union Station. Fare: $3 local, $5.25 regional.

Littleton has excellent light rail access, making it one of the most transit-friendly suburbs in the Denver metro. The Downtown Littleton station puts you steps from Main Street, and the line continues south to the Mineral station with park-and-ride options.

Littleton Area Stations

Downtown Littleton: The most useful station for visitors. Right next to Main Street and historic downtown. Limited parking, but walkable from nearby neighborhoods.

Littleton/Mineral: End of the C line (D line continues to Lone Tree). Large park-and-ride lot. Good option if driving to catch the train.

Oxford - City of Sheridan: Between downtown Littleton and Englewood. Serves the Sheridan area.

The Lines

C Line: Runs from Littleton/Mineral through Denver to Union Station. Continues north to Westminster.

D Line: Similar to C Line but continues south past Mineral to Lone Tree. Good for accessing Park Meadows and the Tech Center.

Both lines run every 15 minutes during peak hours, less frequently evenings and weekends.

To Downtown Denver

From Downtown Littleton station:

  • About 25 minutes to Union Station
  • Trains run frequently during commute hours
  • No transfers needed—direct service
  • $3 local fare covers the trip

This makes Littleton practical for day trips from Denver or as a base for exploring the metro area.

Park and Ride

If you're driving to catch the train:

Littleton/Mineral: Large lot, usually has availability except during special events.

Downtown Littleton: Very limited parking. Street parking fills early.

Parking at RTD lots is free with valid fare.

Tips

Buy tickets before boarding. Use the RTD app or ticket machines at stations. Fare inspectors do check.

Check schedules on weekends. Service is less frequent outside rush hours. The RTD app shows real-time arrivals.

Bikes are welcome. Each train has space for bikes. Good for combining trail rides with transit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay?

RTD app, ticket machines at stations, or contactless payment on newer fare readers. Cash works at machines. Day passes available.

Does light rail go to the airport?

Yes, but you'll need to transfer. Take C or D line to Union Station, then A Line to DEN. About 1 hour total from Littleton.

Is it safe?

Generally yes, especially during daytime and commute hours. Like any transit, use normal urban awareness.

Can I reach Hudson Gardens by light rail?

Not directly. Downtown Littleton station is closest, then about a 20-minute walk or short rideshare to Hudson Gardens.

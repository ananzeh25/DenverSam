Where to Stay in Aurora

Quick Info

Best value: Hotels near DIA are 30-50% cheaper than downtown Denver. Best areas: Airport corridor, Southlands, Stanley Marketplace area. Transit: A Line to downtown Denver. Why Aurora: Budget-friendly with good food scene.

Aurora hotels offer excellent value compared to downtown Denver, especially near the airport. If you're flying in late or out early, Aurora saves both time and money. RTD's A Line connects to Union Station in under an hour, making downtown accessible without the downtown price tag.

Airport Area Hotels

Near DIA

Multiple chain hotels cluster near the airport with free shuttles. Brands include Marriott, Hilton, Hyatt, and budget options. Convenient for flight connections but far from attractions.

Tower Road Corridor

Newer hotels along Tower Road offer good value. Easy airport access with more dining options than the immediate terminal area.

Central Aurora

Near Stanley Marketplace

Hotels in this area put you close to Aurora's best food and shopping. More neighborhood feel than airport hotels. Good R Line access.

I-225 Corridor

Business hotels along I-225 offer meeting facilities and easy highway access. Convenient for exploring both Aurora and Denver.

South Aurora

Near Southlands

Newer hotels near the Southlands shopping area. Suburban feel with restaurants and shopping walkable. Good for families.

Tips

Airport hotels save time. If you have an early flight, staying near DIA eliminates morning stress.

Check shuttle service. Many airport hotels offer free shuttles but schedules vary.

Use the A Line. RTD connects airport hotels to downtown. No need to rent a car just for the trip.

Explore Aurora dining. You're near some of metro Denver's best international food.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth staying near DIA?

For early flights or late arrivals, absolutely. The savings over downtown hotels can be significant.

How do I get to downtown Denver from Aurora?

RTD A Line from airport area or R Line from central Aurora. Both connect to Union Station.

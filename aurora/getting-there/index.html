Getting to Aurora

Quick Info

From DIA: Aurora borders the airport—15-30 minutes depending on destination. From Denver: RTD R Line or I-225. Transit: A Line and R Line serve Aurora. Car needed: Aurora is more spread out than Denver; car helpful but not essential.

Aurora stretches across three counties east of Denver, bordering Denver International Airport. Its proximity to DIA makes it an easy first or last stop for Colorado visitors. RTD light rail connects Aurora to both the airport and downtown Denver.

From Denver International Airport

By Car or Rideshare

Aurora begins immediately west of the airport. Hotel clusters near DIA are 10-15 minutes. Central Aurora (Stanley Marketplace area) is about 20 minutes. South Aurora (Southlands) is 25-30 minutes.

RTD A Line

The A Line connects DIA to downtown Denver, passing through Aurora. Stations at 40th/Airport and Peoria/Smith serve Aurora areas. About $10.50 one-way.

Hotel Shuttles

Many airport-area hotels offer free shuttle service. Check with your hotel before booking rideshare.

From Downtown Denver

RTD R Line

The R Line runs from downtown Denver through Aurora to Lincoln Station. Stops at Nine Mile, Florida, and other Aurora locations. Connected to the broader RTD network.

By Car

I-225 runs north-south through Aurora. Colfax Avenue (US 40) connects east-west. Traffic can be heavy during rush hours.

Getting Around Aurora

RTD Bus Service: Multiple bus routes connect Aurora neighborhoods. Coverage varies by area.

Rideshare: Uber and Lyft operate throughout Aurora. Readily available in populated areas.

Car Rental: Available at DIA and various Aurora locations. Useful for exploring spread-out attractions.

Tips

Aurora is spread out. Unlike walkable downtown Denver, Aurora benefits from wheels.

RTD works for many destinations. Stanley Marketplace, Nine Mile, and central Aurora are accessible by transit.

Airport proximity is convenient. Use Aurora as your base for early departures or late arrivals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get around Aurora without a car?

Possible but limiting. RTD serves major corridors, but some neighborhoods and attractions require wheels.

How long does RTD take to downtown Denver?

R Line from Nine Mile to Union Station is about 35-40 minutes. A Line from airport stations is similar.

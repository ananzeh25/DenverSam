Southlands

Quick Info

What: Open-air shopping and dining center. Location: South Aurora, E-470 and Smoky Hill Road. Stores: 100+ retailers. Vibe: Suburban outdoor mall with main street feel. Best for: Shopping, family dining, movies.

Southlands is Aurora's premier shopping destination, offering an open-air "lifestyle center" with over 100 stores and restaurants. The layout mimics a walkable main street, making it more pleasant than a traditional enclosed mall.

Shopping

Anchor Stores

Major retailers include Macy's, Dick's Sporting Goods, and Bass Pro Shops. Good variety of national brands.

Specialty Shops

Mix of clothing, home goods, and specialty retailers. Better variety than most suburban centers.

Main Street Feel

Outdoor walkways with landscaping create a pleasant shopping environment. Benches and gathering spots throughout.

Dining

Restaurants

Full-service options include P.F. Chang's, Yard House, and local favorites. Range from casual to nicer sit-down meals.

Quick Bites

Fast-casual options throughout. Coffee shops, bakeries, and grab-and-go choices.

Entertainment

AMC Theatre

Multi-screen movie theater with modern amenities. Good option for evening entertainment.

Events

Southlands hosts seasonal events, concerts, and community gatherings. Check their calendar for current offerings.

Tips

Weather-dependent. Outdoor layout means dress for conditions. Colorado sun can be intense.

Parking is plentiful. Large lots surround the center. Free parking throughout.

Good family option. Kid-friendly atmosphere with space to walk around.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Southlands better than Cherry Creek?

Different vibe. Southlands is suburban and family-oriented. Cherry Creek is more upscale and urban. Both serve their purpose.

How far is Southlands from Denver?

About 25-30 minutes from downtown Denver depending on traffic.

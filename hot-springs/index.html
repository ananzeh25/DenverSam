Colorado Hot Springs Guide 2026

Natural mineral springs, geothermal pools & mountain soaking spots

Looking for Hot Springs Near Denver?

Closest option: Indian Hot Springs (35 miles, 45 min). Best overall: Mount Princeton Hot Springs (2.5 hrs, waterslide + creek pools). Most natural: Strawberry Park Hot Springs (3 hrs, stone pools in forest).

Colorado is home to some of the best natural hot springs in the United States. From quick escapes 45 minutes from Denver to world-class resorts in the San Juan Mountains, here's your complete guide to soaking in the Centennial State.

Quick Escapes from Denver (Under 1 Hour)

Indian Hot Springs

35 miles from Denver (45 min)
90-112°F $22-28 Geothermal Caves

The closest true hot springs to Denver. Features unique geothermal caves, a tropical dome pool, and private mineral baths. Perfect for a quick weekday escape.

Weekend Trip Destinations (2-3 Hours)

Mount Princeton Hot Springs

135 miles from Denver (2.5 hrs)
95-105°F $25-30 Waterslide

Resort-style hot springs along Chalk Creek with multiple pools, a waterslide, creekside soaking spots, lodging, spa services, and restaurant.

Full Guide →

Glenwood Hot Springs

157 miles from Denver (2.5 hrs)
90-104°F $32-36 World's Largest

The world's largest hot springs pool - two city blocks of mineral water. Iconic Colorado destination with hotel, spa, and athletic club.

Full Guide →

Strawberry Park Hot Springs

157 miles from Denver (3 hrs)
101-106°F $20 Natural Pools

Rustic natural stone pools surrounded by forest near Steamboat Springs. Clothing-optional after dark. Most authentic Colorado hot springs experience.

Full Guide →

Road Trip Adventures (5+ Hours)

Pagosa Springs

276 miles from Denver (5 hrs)
83-114°F $35-49 World's Deepest

Home to the world's deepest geothermal hot spring (Guinness World Record). Multiple resort options along the San Juan River with stunning mountain views.

Full Guide →

Ouray Hot Springs

366 miles from Denver (6.5 hrs)
88-105°F $18-24 Mountain Views

Soak in the "Switzerland of America" with dramatic mountain peaks surrounding you on all sides. Recently renovated facility with multiple pool sections.

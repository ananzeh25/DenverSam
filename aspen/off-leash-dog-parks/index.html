Aspen Dog Parks

Mountain adventures with your pup

Aspen is a welcoming destination for dogs, with dedicated off-leash areas and miles of dog-friendly trails in the surrounding mountains. Here's where to take your four-legged friend.

Aspen Dog Park

Off-Leash Area

Fenced off-leash dog park near the Aspen Recreation Center. Mountain views and separate areas.

Fenced Mountain Views

Hunter Creek Trail

Off-Leash Hiking

Dogs allowed off-leash on Hunter Creek Trail under voice control. Starts right from downtown Aspen.

Off-Leash Hiking

Rio Grande Trail

On-Leash Path

Dogs welcome on-leash on the paved Rio Grande Trail. Easy walking through the Roaring Fork Valley.

On-Leash Paved

National Forest Trails

Wilderness Hiking

Most White River National Forest trails allow dogs off-leash. Note: Dogs not allowed at Maroon Bells shuttle area.

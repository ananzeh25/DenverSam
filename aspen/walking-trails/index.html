Aspen Hiking Trails

World-class trails in the Elk Mountains

Aspen is surrounded by some of Colorado's most stunning hiking terrain. From the iconic Maroon Bells trails to challenging 14er climbs, the Elk Mountains offer adventures for every ability level.

Maroon Bells Scenic Loop

The Classic

Easy 1.8-mile loop around Maroon Lake with stunning Bells views. Colorado's most photographed trail.

1.8 mi Easy

Hunter Creek Trail

From Downtown

Popular trail starting from downtown Aspen. Multiple loop options through aspen groves and meadows.

Various Moderate

American Lake

Alpine Lake Destination

7-mile roundtrip to a beautiful alpine lake. Excellent wildflowers in July and fewer crowds than Maroon Bells.

7 mi RT Moderate

Smuggler Mountain Road

Town Overlook

Steep climb to the mining-era observation deck with 360-degree views of Aspen and the Elk Mountains.

3 mi RT Moderate

Rio Grande Trail

Paved Valley Path

Paved trail from Aspen to Glenwood Springs through the Roaring Fork Valley. Great for biking.

Paved Easy

Conundrum Hot Springs

Backcountry Hot Springs

17-mile roundtrip to natural hot springs at 11,200 feet. Permit required. Unforgettable overnight trip.

17 mi RT Hard

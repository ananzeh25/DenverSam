Aspen Parks

Mountain town green spaces

While the surrounding wilderness is Aspen's main attraction, the town maintains several charming parks perfect for events, picnics, and enjoying the mountain setting.

Wagner Park

Downtown Green

Downtown park with volleyball, events lawn, and Aspen Mountain views. Host of many summer festivals.

Events Downtown

Paepcke Park

Aspen Institute Grounds

Beautiful grounds of the Aspen Institute with mature trees and mountain views. Peaceful retreat.

Koch Lumber Park

River Access

Riverside park with picnic areas and Roaring Fork River access. Popular for summer swimming.

River Picnic

Mountain Golf

18-hole municipal course with stunning mountain scenery. Also offers winter Nordic skiing.

Aspen Restaurants

World-class dining at 7,908 feet

Aspen's restaurant scene rivals major cities, with celebrity chefs, James Beard winners, and legendary establishments. From après ski to special occasion dining, here are Aspen's essential restaurants.

Matsuhisa Aspen

Nobu's Original Mountain Location

Chef Nobu Matsuhisa's legendary Japanese-Peruvian cuisine in an intimate Aspen setting. Book well ahead.

$$$$ Japanese

Casa Tua

Italian Excellence

Sophisticated Italian with impeccable service. Members club upstairs, restaurant for all. The Aspen elite's choice.

$$$$ Italian

Ajax Tavern

Après Ski Institution

The place to see and be seen after skiing. French-American cuisine at the base of Aspen Mountain.

$$$ Après Ski

Clark's Oyster Bar

Fresh Seafood

East coast-style oyster bar in the mountains. Excellent raw bar, lobster rolls, and lively atmosphere.

$$$ Seafood

White House Tavern

Historic Charmer

Casual elegance in a historic Aspen home. Excellent salads, sandwiches, and afternoon cocktails.

$$ Casual

Pine Creek Cookhouse

Backcountry Dining

Ski, snowshoe, or horse-drawn sleigh to this backcountry restaurant. Unforgettable mountain experience.

$$$$ Unique

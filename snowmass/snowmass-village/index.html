Snowmass Village

Quick Info

Character: Family-friendly ski village. Access: Ski-in/ski-out from base area. Vibe: Relaxed compared to Aspen. Shuttle: Free connection to Aspen (20 minutes).

Snowmass Village offers a different experience from Aspen proper. Where Aspen has historic charm and celebrity culture, Snowmass has family-friendly convenience and a more laid-back atmosphere. The village sits at the base of the ski mountain, with most lodging offering ski-in/ski-out access or a short walk to the lifts.

The Base Village

The central base area underwent significant redevelopment in recent years:

  • Modern pedestrian plaza at the base of the gondola
  • Ice skating rink in winter
  • Restaurants and shops lining the plaza
  • Direct access to the Treehouse kids' center

The development brought contemporary architecture and amenities while keeping the family focus that defines Snowmass.

Snowmass Mall

The older Mall area, located slightly uphill from the base village, offers:

  • More established restaurants and bars
  • Grocery store for condos and self-catering
  • Ski shops and rentals
  • A different, more traditional ski village feel

The two areas connect via walking paths and the free village shuttle.

Dining

Snowmass dining is more casual than Aspen:

Base Village: Contemporary options including Slice, Mix6, and upscale choices at the Limelight Hotel.

Mall area: Established spots like Venga Venga (Mexican) and Brothers Grille (casual American).

On-mountain: Elk Camp offers elevated mountain dining. Sam's Smokehouse serves BBQ at a mid-mountain location.

Snowmass vs. Aspen

Stay in Snowmass if:

  • You're traveling with kids
  • Ski-in/ski-out matters
  • You prefer a quieter, more relaxed atmosphere
  • Budget is a consideration (generally lower prices)

Stay in Aspen if:

  • Nightlife and dining are priorities
  • You want the historic town experience
  • Shopping and culture matter

The free shuttle makes it easy to enjoy both. Many families base in Snowmass and visit Aspen for dinners.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Snowmass from Aspen?

About 9 miles, roughly 20 minutes by free shuttle or car. Shuttles run frequently throughout the day and evening.

Is Snowmass walkable?

The base village and mall areas are both walkable. Walking between them involves a hill. The free village shuttle connects everything.

Are there groceries in Snowmass?

Yes. A grocery store in the mall area serves condo guests. For larger shopping, Aspen has more options.

What about nightlife?

Limited compared to Aspen. A few bars and après options, but the village quiets down earlier. Take the shuttle to Aspen for more nightlife.

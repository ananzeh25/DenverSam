A convenient alternative to Denver

Distance to Downtown: 10 miles

Transfer Time: 15-25 minutes to most destinations

Why COS? Sometimes cheaper than DIA with less crowding. Great option if Colorado Springs is your destination or you want to avoid Denver traffic.

COS vs Denver Airport

When to choose Colorado Springs

Choose COS If:

  • Your destination is in the Pikes Peak region
  • Flights are comparable in price (sometimes cheaper)
  • You want a smaller, less hectic airport
  • You're visiting Garden of the Gods, Pikes Peak, Air Force Academy
  • You're heading to Cripple Creek casinos

Choose DIA If:

  • More flight options needed
  • Heading to Denver or north
  • International connections
  • Better fare significantly

COS Advantages

  • Smaller: Easier navigation, shorter security lines
  • Growing: More routes being added
  • Location: 10 miles to downtown vs 25+ for DIA to Denver
  • Cost: Sometimes significantly cheaper flights

Transportation Options

Getting from COS to your destination

Uber/Lyft ($20-35)

The easiest option for most travelers. Good availability at COS.

  • Pick up at designated rideshare area
  • 15-20 minutes to downtown
  • 25-30 minutes to Garden of the Gods area
  • Generally reliable availability

Taxi ($30-45)

Available at the airport taxi stand. Fixed rates to common destinations.

  • Yellow Cab, Springs Cab companies
  • No surge pricing
  • Good for those without smartphone apps

Hotel Shuttles (Free-$15)

Many Colorado Springs hotels offer airport pickup. Call ahead to arrange.

  • The Broadmoor offers luxury shuttle service
  • Many chain hotels have courtesy vans
  • Confirm with your hotel when booking

Rental Car ($50-100/day)

Recommended if you want to explore the region. Colorado Springs is spread out.

  • All major companies at the airport
  • Useful for Garden of the Gods, Pikes Peak, Manitou
  • Essential for Cripple Creek day trip
  • Lower rates than Denver typically

Private Car Service ($60-100)

For business travelers or those wanting guaranteed service.

  • Book in advance
  • Meet-and-greet available
  • Luxury vehicles available

Do You Need a Car?

Unlike Denver or Boulder, Colorado Springs is spread out and less transit-friendly. A rental car is recommended if you want to explore Garden of the Gods, Pikes Peak, or Manitou Springs efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Colorado Springs Airport transportation

Is there Uber at Colorado Springs Airport?

Yes, both Uber and Lyft operate at COS. Pick up at the designated rideshare area outside baggage claim. Expect $20-35 to most Colorado Springs destinations.

Should I fly into Colorado Springs or Denver?

Colorado Springs if that's your destination—you'll save 60+ miles of driving. Denver if you need more flight options or are heading north. Compare fares; COS is sometimes cheaper.

How far is Colorado Springs Airport from Denver?

About 70 miles, 75-90 minutes driving on I-25. Not practical as a Denver alternative unless COS flights are significantly cheaper.

Do I need a rental car in Colorado Springs?

Recommended. Colorado Springs is spread out and public transit is limited. You'll want a car for Garden of the Gods, Pikes Peak, Manitou Springs, and day trips.

Is there a shuttle to Cripple Creek from COS?

No direct airport shuttle. Rent a car or take rideshare to town, then use a casino shuttle from Colorado Springs to Cripple Creek. Or rent a car for the scenic drive.

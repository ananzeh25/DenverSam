Colorado Springs Lakes

Water escapes in the Pikes Peak region

While Colorado Springs is known for its red rocks and mountain scenery, several lakes and reservoirs offer great fishing, kayaking, and relaxation. From urban parks to mountain reservoirs, here are the best water spots near Colorado Springs.

Quail Lake

South Colorado Springs

Urban lake with walking trail, fishing, and Pikes Peak views. Popular for morning walks and kayaking.

Kayaking Trail

Prospect Lake

Memorial Park

Lake in Memorial Park with fishing, paddleboating, and the iconic Skirvin Pool. Great for families.

Fishing Family

Rampart Reservoir

Pike National Forest

Mountain reservoir with excellent fishing, kayaking, and 12-mile loop trail. Beautiful high-altitude escape.

Mountain 12 mi trail

North Catamount Reservoir

Pikes Peak Highway

Stunning mountain reservoir on the Pikes Peak Highway. Incredible scenery, fishing, and peaceful atmosphere.

Scenic Fishing

Manitou Lake

Woodland Park Area

Mountain lake near Woodland Park with camping, fishing, and hiking. Great for a day trip from the Springs.

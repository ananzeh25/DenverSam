Colorado Springs Hiking Trails

Red rocks to summit views

Colorado Springs offers some of Colorado's most iconic hikes. From the famous Incline to the trails winding through Garden of the Gods, the hiking here ranges from easy red rock walks to challenging mountain ascents.

The Incline

Manitou Springs Challenge

2,744 steps, 2,000 feet of elevation gain in less than a mile. Brutal, famous, and unforgettable.

Extreme 1 mi

Garden of the Gods Trails

Red Rock Wonders

15+ miles of trails through towering red rock formations. Perimeter Trail, Central Garden, and more.

Easy-Moderate Free

Barr Trail

Pikes Peak Summit

13-mile trail to the summit of Pikes Peak. Epic 14er hike with 7,500 feet of elevation gain.

Hard 26 mi RT

Red Rock Canyon

Open Space

Less crowded alternative to Garden of the Gods. Similar red rock scenery with hiking, biking, and climbing.

Moderate Biking

North Cheyenne Canyon

Waterfall Country

Forested canyon with waterfalls, multiple trails, and Helen Hunt Falls. Free park near Broadmoor.

Waterfalls Free

Palmer Park

Urban Mountain Park

730 acres of trails in the city with red rock formations and Pikes Peak views. Popular for trail running.

Trail Running 730 acres

Waldo Canyon

Recovering Landscape

7-mile loop with views of Pikes Peak. Recovering from 2012 fire but offers unique landscape views.

Moderate 7 mi loop

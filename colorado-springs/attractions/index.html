Colorado Springs Attractions

Natural wonders at the base of Pikes Peak

Colorado Springs sits at the foot of Pikes Peak, surrounded by dramatic red rock formations and stunning mountain scenery. From the world-famous Garden of the Gods to historic Manitou Springs, here are the essential Colorado Springs experiences.

Garden of the Gods

Colorado's Most Famous Park

Towering red rock formations against Pikes Peak. Free admission, hiking trails, rock climbing, and stunning photo ops.

Free Hiking

Pikes Peak

America's Mountain

14,115 feet of inspiration. Drive, hike, or take the cog railway to the summit for 360-degree views.

14er Cog Railway

Manitou Springs

Quirky Mountain Town

Historic mineral springs town with galleries, shops, and restaurants. Gateway to the Incline and Barr Trail.

Springs Shopping

Cheyenne Mountain Zoo

America's Only Mountain Zoo

Mountain zoo at 6,800 feet with giraffe feeding and stunning views. Consistently rated among America's best.

Zoo Family

The Broadmoor

Legendary Resort

Historic five-star resort with golf, spa, dining, and Seven Falls. Worth visiting even without staying.

Resort Golf

Seven Falls

Spectacular Waterfall

224 steps to the top of seven cascading waterfalls. Night lighting and hiking trails in a stunning canyon.

Waterfall Hiking

U.S. Olympic Training Center

Olympic City USA

Tour the U.S. Olympic & Paralympic Training Center. Free tours and visitor center celebrating American athletes.

Free Tours Olympics

U.S. Air Force Academy

Military Excellence

Visit the stunning Cadet Chapel and visitor center. Free admission to public areas of this prestigious institution.

Free Chapel

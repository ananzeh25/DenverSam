Colorado Springs Dog Parks

Off-leash fun in Olympic City

Colorado Springs has excellent off-leash options for dogs, from dedicated dog parks to expansive open spaces where your pup can run free with stunning mountain views.

Bear Creek Dog Park

Colorado Springs' Best

25 acres of off-leash trails, water features, and open space. Separate areas for large and small dogs.

25 acres Water

Palmer Park Dog Area

Mountain Views

Off-leash area within Palmer Park with trails and red rock scenery. Great for active dogs who love to hike.

Trails Scenic

Rampart Dog Park

Neighborhood Favorite

Well-maintained fenced dog park with separate areas. Popular with locals on the north side.

Fenced Separate Areas

Garden of the Gods (Leash Required)

Scenic Walking

Dogs welcome on-leash throughout the park. Beautiful trails among red rocks with Pikes Peak views.

On-Leash Scenic

Red Rock Canyon Dog Area

Open Space

Dogs allowed off-leash in designated areas. Similar scenery to Garden of the Gods with fewer crowds.

Belmar

Quick Info

Type: Walkable downtown district. Features: Restaurants, shops, movies, events. Events: Farmers market, ice skating, concerts. Location: Alameda Ave and Wadsworth. Parking: Free garages and street. Best for: Dining, shopping, entertainment.

Belmar is Lakewood's downtown—a walkable mixed-use district built on the site of the former Villa Italia Mall. The development creates an urban village feel with streets, plazas, restaurants, and shops.

Dining

Restaurants

Diverse dining options from casual to upscale. Good variety for different tastes and occasions.

Coffee & Dessert

Coffee shops and bakeries for morning fuel or afternoon breaks.

Outdoor Patios

Many restaurants offer patio seating—pleasant in Colorado's mild weather.

Shopping

Retail

Mix of national chains and local boutiques. Clothing, home goods, specialty shops.

Target

Anchor retailer for everyday essentials.

Entertainment

Movie Theater

Multi-screen cinema for first-run films.

Events

Summer concerts, holiday events, and community activities throughout the year.

Seasonal Events

Farmers Market

Summer farmers market with local produce, food vendors, and artisan goods.

Ice Skating

Outdoor ice skating rink in winter. Festive holiday atmosphere.

Tips

Free parking. Garages and street parking available at no charge.

Walk around. The district is designed for walking. Explore on foot.

Check events. Summer and holiday seasons have special programming.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happened to Villa Italia?

The enclosed mall was demolished and replaced with Belmar, a new urbanist development.

Is Belmar a mall?

No—it's an outdoor urban district with streets and sidewalks, not an enclosed mall.

Is there good dining?

Yes—Belmar has become Lakewood's dining hub with diverse options.

When is the farmers market?

Summer season, typically Saturday mornings. Check schedule for exact dates.

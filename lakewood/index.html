Lakewood, Colorado

Gateway to the Foothills

Your Guide to Lakewood

Urban living meets mountain access

Lakewood sits between Denver and the foothills, offering the best of both worlds. The city touches the mountains at its western edge, where trails lead into the high country. Downtown Belmar provides walkable urban energy.

Casa Bonita—the iconic Mexican restaurant with cliff divers, caves, and mariachi—reopened after a major renovation by South Park creators Trey Parker and Matt Stone. It's become a Colorado destination in its own right.

Bear Creek Lake Park offers hiking, swimming, and fishing just 20 minutes from downtown Denver. The park connects to trails leading into the foothills for mountain biking and longer hikes.

Explore Lakewood

Parks, dining, and mountain access

ICON

Casa Bonita

The legendary restaurant with cliff divers, caves, and puppet shows. Now revamped with elevated Mexican cuisine after South Park creators took over.

TOWN

Belmar

Walkable downtown district with restaurants, shops, and a movie theater. Farmers market in summer, ice skating in winter.

PARK

Bear Creek Lake Park

2,600 acres with swimming beach, hiking, biking, and fishing. Connects to foothills trails for longer adventures.

TRAIL

Green Mountain

Popular hiking and mountain biking destination with Denver skyline views. Multiple trails for all skill levels.

BIKE

Bear Creek Trail

Paved path connecting to Denver's trail system. Runs along Bear Creek with access to parks and neighborhoods.

ART

40 West Arts

Creative district along West Colfax with galleries, studios, and public art. First Friday art walks and community events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Planning your Lakewood visit

How do I get tickets to Casa Bonita?

Reservations are required and released online. They sell out quickly—check the website regularly and book as soon as tickets drop.

How far is Lakewood from Denver?

Lakewood borders Denver on the west. Belmar is about 15 minutes from downtown Denver; the foothills parks are 20-25 minutes.

Is Bear Creek Lake Park worth the entrance fee?

Yes, especially in summer. The beach, trails, and foothills access make it one of the best parks in the metro area.

Can I access the mountains from Lakewood?

Absolutely. Lakewood is the gateway to I-70 and the mountain corridor. Golden is 10 minutes away; ski resorts about 90 minutes.

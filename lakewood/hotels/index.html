Lakewood Hotels

Quick Info

Location: West Denver metro. Hotel areas: Federal Center, Union corridor, Belmar area. Access: 15 min to downtown Denver, 20 min to mountains. Price range: Budget to mid-range. Best for: Federal Center business, mountain access, value.

Lakewood offers affordable hotel options west of Denver with easy access to both downtown and the mountains. The city's location makes it a practical base for exploring the metro area or heading into the foothills.

Hotel Areas

Federal Center Area

Hotels near the Denver Federal Center serve government contractors and visitors. Good access to US-6 and C-470 for regional travel.

Union Boulevard

Cluster of hotels along Union Boulevard with chain options at various price points. Easy I-70 access for mountain travel.

Belmar Area

Hotels near Belmar offer walkable dining and entertainment. Good balance of convenience and character.

Hotel Types

Chain Hotels

Major chains including Marriott, Hilton, and IHG properties. Consistent amenities and loyalty program options.

Budget Options

Affordable options along Colfax and major corridors. Basic amenities at lower price points.

Extended Stay

Extended stay properties for longer visits. Kitchen facilities for self-catering.

Common Amenities

Parking

Most Lakewood hotels offer free parking—a significant advantage over downtown Denver.

Breakfast

Many properties include complimentary breakfast.

Fitness

Fitness centers and pools at mid-range and upper properties.

Tips

Mountain staging. Lakewood is a good base for early mountain departures. Skip downtown traffic.

Federal Center visitors. Book nearby for easy access without downtown commute.

Free parking. Save on downtown parking costs by staying in Lakewood.

Light rail access. W Line connects Lakewood to downtown Denver for car-free city visits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Lakewood from downtown Denver?

About 10-15 miles depending on location. 15-25 minutes by car, 30-40 minutes by light rail.

Is Lakewood a good base for skiing?

Yes—closer to I-70 mountain access than downtown Denver, with free hotel parking.

Are there hotels near Casa Bonita?

Several hotels within 5-10 minutes of Casa Bonita in the central Lakewood area.

Is there public transit?

Yes—RTD W Line light rail connects Lakewood to downtown Denver.

40 West Arts District

Quick Info

Location: West Colfax corridor. Features: Galleries, studios, murals. Events: First Friday art walks. Vibe: Creative, eclectic, emerging. State designation: Certified Creative District. Best for: Art lovers, gallery hopping.

40 West Arts is Lakewood's state-certified creative district—a stretch of West Colfax featuring galleries, artist studios, public murals, and creative businesses. The district has transformed the corridor into a vibrant arts destination.

Galleries & Studios

Gallery Scene

Multiple galleries showcase local and regional artists. Mix of contemporary art, traditional work, and experimental pieces.

Artist Studios

Working artists have studios throughout the district. Some offer open hours for visitors to see work in progress.

Creative Businesses

Art supply stores, frame shops, and creative services cluster in the district.

Public Art

Murals

Large-scale murals enliven building walls throughout the corridor. New works appear regularly as part of ongoing arts initiatives.

Sculptures

Public sculptures and installations add dimension to the streetscape.

Events

First Friday Art Walks

Monthly art walks on the first Friday of each month. Galleries open late, new exhibits debut, and artists mingle with visitors.

Special Events

Art festivals, markets, and community events throughout the year.

Food & Drink

Restaurants

Casual dining options along Colfax. Good spots for a meal before or after gallery visits.

Coffee

Coffee shops provide fuel for art exploration.

Tips

First Friday. Best time to visit for the full experience. Galleries open late with opening receptions.

Walk the corridor. The district is spread along Colfax. Park and walk to see everything.

Check gallery hours. Hours vary by gallery. Some are by appointment only.

Support artists. Many works are for sale at accessible prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 40 West?

A state-certified creative district along West Colfax in Lakewood featuring galleries, studios, and public art.

When is the art walk?

First Friday of each month, typically evening hours. Check the 40 West website for details.

Is it free?

Yes—galleries are free to visit. First Friday events are free.

Is there parking?

Street parking available along Colfax. Some lots near galleries.

Arvada Craft Breweries

Quick Info

Top brewery: New Image Brewing. Concentration: Several in/near Olde Town. Style: Self-guided tours work well. Access: G Line makes brewery hopping car-free. Best for: Craft beer enthusiasts, casual visits.

Arvada has emerged as a legitimate craft beer destination, anchored by nationally-acclaimed New Image Brewing. Several breweries within walking or short driving distance make for excellent self-guided brewery tours.

The G Line connection to Denver means you can visit Arvada breweries without driving—take the train, walk between spots, and train home. Responsible drinking made easy.

The Breweries

New Image Brewing

The flagship Arvada brewery with national recognition. Known for hazy IPAs and creative releases. Modern taproom with regular new beer releases. Often has lines for special releases—that's how good it is.

Known for: Hazy IPAs, sours, creative styles.

Vibe: Modern, popular, beer-focused.

Odyssey Beerwerks

Award-winning brewery in Olde Town. Wide range of styles from traditional to experimental. Good food truck presence and events.

Known for: Variety, quality across styles.

Vibe: Welcoming, community-oriented.

Additional Options

Several other breweries and taprooms operate in Arvada. The scene continues to grow. Check current listings for the latest openings.

Self-Guided Brewery Tour

Olde Town Circuit

Start at one Olde Town brewery, walk to another, grab food at a local restaurant between. The compact downtown makes this easy.

Expanded Tour

Some breweries are outside Olde Town proper. With a designated driver or ride-share, you can expand the tour to cover more ground.

G Line Strategy

Take the G Line from Denver. Walk to breweries near Olde Town station. Train home. No driving, no parking, no concerns.

Food Options

Some breweries have food trucks or on-site food. Olde Town restaurants are walking distance for proper meals between brewery stops. Plan accordingly.

Tips

Check release schedules. New Image's special releases draw crowds. Check their social media for drop times if you want specific beers.

Take the train. The G Line makes responsible brewery hopping easy. No need to designate a driver.

Pace yourself. Colorado altitude plus craft beer strength adds up. Drink water, eat food, take your time.

Weekend afternoons. Best time for brewery visits. Most are open by early afternoon and stay lively into evening.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best brewery in Arvada?

New Image gets the most acclaim and is worth seeking out for serious beer enthusiasts. Odyssey and others are also excellent.

Can I walk between breweries?

In Olde Town, yes. Some breweries are spread out and require driving or ride-share.

Are kids allowed?

Policies vary. Many breweries are family-friendly during daytime hours. Check individual policies.

Do I need reservations?

Generally no for regular visits. Special release events may have different rules.

How does Arvada compare to other Colorado beer scenes?

Smaller than Denver or Fort Collins, but New Image gives it credibility. Worth a visit for craft beer fans.

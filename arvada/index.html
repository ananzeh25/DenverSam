Arvada, Colorado

Historic Heart, Modern Soul

Your Guide to Arvada

Olde Town charm and outdoor access

Arvada's Olde Town is one of the Denver metro's best-kept secrets. Historic buildings house craft breweries, local restaurants, and boutique shops. The walkable downtown feels like a small Colorado town despite being minutes from Denver.

The G Line light rail now connects Arvada directly to Union Station, making Olde Town an easy evening destination from anywhere in Denver. New West Station brought additional development and dining options.

Outdoor recreation abounds. Ralston Creek Trail winds through the city, connecting to regional trail systems. Two Ponds National Wildlife Refuge offers birding and nature walks. Apex Park is minutes away for hiking and mountain biking.

Explore Arvada

History, brewing, and trails

TOWN

Olde Town

Historic downtown with local restaurants, craft breweries, and boutique shops. Farmers market and festivals year-round.

BEER

Craft Breweries

New Image, Odyssey, and other breweries call Arvada home. Self-guided brewery tours through Olde Town and beyond.

TRAIL

Ralston Creek Trail

Paved trail connecting Arvada to regional systems. Walk, bike, or run through parks and neighborhoods.

HIKE

Apex Park

Foothills hiking and mountain biking just west of Arvada. Part of Jefferson County Open Space with stunning views.

BIRD

Two Ponds Wildlife Refuge

Urban wildlife refuge with walking trails and birding. Over 100 bird species documented in this 72-acre sanctuary.

TRAIN

G Line

Light rail connecting Olde Town to downtown Denver. Makes Arvada an easy destination without driving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Planning your Arvada visit

How do I get to Olde Town Arvada?

Take the G Line light rail to Olde Town station, or drive via Wadsworth Boulevard or I-70. Parking is available in downtown lots.

What's the best brewery in Arvada?

New Image gets the most acclaim, but Odyssey and others are worth visiting. A self-guided brewery tour makes for a great afternoon.

Is Arvada close to the mountains?

Yes. Apex Park offers immediate foothills access. Golden is 10 minutes away; ski resorts about 75 minutes via I-70.

When is the farmers market?

Sunday mornings from June through September in Olde Town. One of the better farmers markets in the metro area.

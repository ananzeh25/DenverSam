Central City Casinos

Quick Info

Style: Smaller casinos in historic buildings. Atmosphere: More intimate than Black Hawk's mega-resorts. Games: Slots, table games, poker. Age requirement: 21+. Difference from Black Hawk: Fewer casinos, more Victorian charm.

Central City's casinos offer a different experience than neighboring Black Hawk. Gaming happens in smaller venues housed in restored Victorian buildings, creating an intimate atmosphere that feels more like gambling in a historic saloon than a Vegas-style resort.

Gaming Options

Slots

The primary gaming option. From penny slots to higher-limit machines. Fewer total machines than Black Hawk but solid variety.

Table Games

Blackjack, craps, roulette, and poker available at some locations. Smaller minimums often available compared to Vegas.

Poker

Some casinos offer poker rooms with regular tournaments and cash games.

The Central City Difference

Historic Buildings

Casinos occupy restored 19th-century structures. Original architecture and details preserved. Gaming in a unique setting.

Smaller Scale

More walkable than Black Hawk's spread-out casinos. Easier to stroll between venues and explore the historic district.

Less Crowded

Generally quieter than Black Hawk, especially on weekdays. More relaxed pace for gaming.

Tips

Walk the main street. Central City's casinos are close together and easy to explore on foot.

Set a budget. Standard gambling advice applies—decide what you're willing to lose before playing.

Use the shuttle. Free shuttle to Black Hawk lets you experience both gaming districts.

Combine with opera. Summer visitors can catch a performance and try the tables.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Central City casinos worth visiting if Black Hawk is bigger?

Yes for the atmosphere. If you want Vegas-style amenities, Black Hawk is better. For historic charm and smaller venues, Central City is the choice.

What's the minimum age?

21 for casino floors, same as all Colorado gaming.

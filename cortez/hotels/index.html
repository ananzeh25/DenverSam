Cortez Hotels

Quick Info

Best for Mesa Verde: Holiday Inn Express, Hampton Inn. Historic: Far View Lodge (inside park). Budget: Chain motels on Main Street. Book ahead: Summer season fills quickly. Alternative: Durango, 45 min east.

Cortez is the closest town to Mesa Verde National Park, making it the most convenient base for exploring the cliff dwellings. Most lodging is along Main Street with familiar chain hotels.

Recommended Hotels

Holiday Inn Express & Suites

Reliable chain option with breakfast included. Pool and fitness center. Good Mesa Verde staging point.

Hampton Inn Cortez

Newer hotel with modern amenities. Free breakfast, pool. One of Cortez's better options.

Best Western Turquoise Inn

Southwestern character with comfortable rooms. Pool. Popular with Mesa Verde visitors.

Inside Mesa Verde

Far View Lodge

The only lodging inside Mesa Verde National Park. Stunning views and sunset access. Book well in advance for summer. Seasonal operation (April-October).

Budget Options

Several budget motels along Main Street offer basic accommodations at lower prices. Read reviews carefully—quality varies.

Tips

Book summer early. Mesa Verde tourism peaks June-August. Hotels fill weeks ahead.

Consider Far View Lodge. Staying inside the park means more time at the ruins and spectacular sunsets.

Durango has more options. 45 minutes east with more restaurants and lodging variety.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Cortez from Mesa Verde?

About 10 miles to the park entrance. The cliff dwellings are another 20 miles inside.

Should I stay in Cortez or Durango?

Cortez is closer to Mesa Verde. Durango has more dining and entertainment options.

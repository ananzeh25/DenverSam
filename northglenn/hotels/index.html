Northglenn Hotels

Quick Info

Location: North Denver metro. Access: I-25, N Line light rail. To downtown Denver: 15-20 minutes. To DIA: 25-30 minutes. Price range: Budget to mid-range. Best for: Value lodging, north metro access.

Northglenn offers affordable hotel options along the I-25 corridor with convenient access to downtown Denver, the airport, and north metro attractions. The N Line provides car-free access to the city center.

Location Advantages

I-25 Access

Hotels cluster near I-25 exits for easy north-south travel. Quick access to downtown Denver or Boulder.

Airport Access

Closer to DIA than many Denver hotels. 25-30 minutes to the airport via E-470 or I-25/I-70.

N Line Transit

Light rail station provides car-free downtown access. Park at the hotel and take the train.

Hotel Types

Chain Hotels

Major chain properties including budget and mid-range brands. Consistent amenities and loyalty programs.

Budget Options

Affordable options for cost-conscious travelers. Basic amenities at lower prices.

Extended Stay

Properties with kitchen facilities for longer visits.

Common Amenities

Parking

Free parking at most Northglenn hotels—significant savings over downtown.

Breakfast

Many properties include complimentary breakfast.

Pools

Some properties offer pools and fitness centers.

Tips

Use N Line. Park at the hotel and take light rail downtown to avoid parking costs.

Book direct. Sometimes better rates booking directly with the hotel.

Check reviews. Quality varies. Read recent reviews before booking.

Airport trips. Good base for early morning flights to DIA.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Northglenn from downtown Denver?

About 12-15 miles. 15-25 minutes by car, 25 minutes by N Line.

Is there public transit?

Yes—N Line light rail connects to Union Station in downtown Denver.

Are Northglenn hotels cheaper than Denver?

Generally yes—suburban rates plus free parking provide savings.

How far to the airport?

25-30 minutes to DIA via E-470 or I-25/I-70.

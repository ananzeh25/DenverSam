Northglenn Recreation Center

Quick Info

Type: Municipal recreation center. Features: Indoor pool, fitness center, gym. Programs: Fitness classes, swim lessons, youth programs. Fees: Day passes and memberships. Resident discounts: Yes. Best for: Fitness, swimming, family activities.

Northglenn Recreation Center provides indoor fitness and aquatic facilities for residents and visitors. The center offers modern equipment, pool facilities, and a variety of programs for all ages.

Aquatics

Indoor Pool

Indoor pool for lap swimming, recreational swimming, and water activities. Temperature-controlled for year-round use.

Swim Lessons

Swim lesson programs for children and adults at various skill levels.

Water Fitness

Water aerobics and aqua fitness classes for low-impact exercise.

Fitness

Fitness Center

Cardio equipment, weight machines, and free weights. Modern equipment in a clean facility.

Group Fitness

Fitness classes including yoga, aerobics, strength training, and more. Check schedule for class times.

Gymnasium

Indoor gym space for basketball, volleyball, and other activities.

Programs

Youth Programs

Programs for kids including camps, sports, and activities throughout the year.

Senior Programs

Fitness and social programs tailored for older adults.

Special Events

Seasonal programs and special events for families and community members.

Tips

Day passes available. Drop in for single visits without membership.

Resident rates. Northglenn residents receive discounted pricing.

Check class schedule. Group fitness classes run on a set schedule.

Pool hours vary. Lap swim and open swim have designated times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a resident?

No—non-residents welcome. Residents receive discounted rates.

Is there a day pass?

Yes—day passes available for single visits.

Are there swim lessons?

Yes—swim lessons for all ages and skill levels.

What are the hours?

Hours vary by day. Check the city website for current schedule.

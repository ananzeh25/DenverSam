Pirate Fest

Quick Info

When: Summer annually. Where: E.B. Rains Jr. Memorial Park. Theme: Pirate celebration. Activities: Entertainment, vendors, food, family fun. Cost: Free admission. Best for: Families, community celebration.

Pirate Fest is Northglenn's signature summer festival—a pirate-themed celebration at E.B. Rains Jr. Memorial Park featuring live entertainment, vendors, food, and family activities. The event draws the community together for a day of swashbuckling fun.

Entertainment

Live Performances

Music, shows, and entertainment throughout the festival. Pirate-themed performers add to the atmosphere.

Kids Activities

Activities and entertainment designed for children. Face painting, games, and interactive experiences.

Costume Contest

Dress as a pirate and join the fun. Costume contests for kids and adults.

Food & Vendors

Food Vendors

Festival food from local vendors. Variety of options for all tastes.

Craft Vendors

Local artisans and craft vendors with unique goods.

Beer Garden

Adult beverages available in designated area.

Location & Parking

E.B. Rains Park

Festival takes place at Northglenn's main park. Beautiful lakeside setting for the celebration.

Parking

Parking available at the park and nearby areas. Arrive early for best spots on busy festival days.

Tips

Arrive early. Parking fills up as the day progresses.

Bring sunscreen. Summer sun can be intense. Hats and shade recommended.

Dress up. Pirate costumes welcome and encouraged.

Bring cash. Some vendors may be cash-only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pirate Fest free?

Yes—free admission. Food and vendor purchases are separate.

When is Pirate Fest?

Summer annually—check Northglenn's event calendar for specific dates.

Is it good for kids?

Yes—family-friendly event with lots of activities for children.

Should I wear a costume?

Costumes are welcome but not required. Many attendees dress in pirate gear.

E.B. Rains Jr. Memorial Park

Quick Info

Size: 100+ acres. Features: Lake, trails, playground, sports fields. Events: Pirate Fest, concerts, community events. Fishing: Yes, stocked lake. Fee: Free. Best for: Families, fishing, community events.

E.B. Rains Jr. Memorial Park is Northglenn's central park—a 100+ acre space featuring a lake, trails, playgrounds, and sports facilities. The park serves as the city's primary gathering place for community events and outdoor recreation.

The Lake

Fishing

The lake is stocked with fish and open for fishing. Popular for shore fishing with family-friendly access.

Wildlife

Ducks, geese, and other waterfowl make the lake home. Peaceful spot for nature observation.

Walking Path

Paved path circles the lake for easy walking, running, or strolling.

Facilities

Playground

Large playground area with equipment for various ages. Popular with families.

Sports Fields

Ball fields and open space for sports and activities.

Picnic Areas

Picnic shelters and tables throughout the park. Some reservable for groups.

Trails

Paved trails connect different areas of the park and link to the broader trail system.

Events

Pirate Fest

Northglenn's signature summer festival held at the park. Pirate-themed fun, vendors, entertainment.

Concerts

Summer concert series and community events throughout the year.

Farmers Market

Seasonal farmers market brings local produce and goods.

Tips

Arrive early for events. Popular events fill parking quickly.

Bring fishing gear. Great spot for teaching kids to fish.

Walk the loop. The lake loop is an easy, scenic walk.

Check event calendar. The city schedules many events at the park.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is fishing free?

Yes, the lake is free to fish. Standard Colorado fishing license required for adults.

Are dogs allowed?

Yes, on leash throughout the park.

When is Pirate Fest?

Summer annually—check Northglenn's event calendar for specific dates.

Is there parking?

Free parking lots throughout the park.

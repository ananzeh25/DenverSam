Northglenn Outdoor Pool

Quick Info

Type: Outdoor municipal pool. Season: Summer (Memorial Day to Labor Day). Features: Slides, splash features, lap lanes. Fees: Daily admission. Resident discounts: Yes. Best for: Family summer swimming.

Northglenn's outdoor pool provides summer swimming fun for families and community members. The facility features water slides, splash features, and space for both recreational swimming and lap swimming.

Pool Features

Water Slides

Water slides for kids and families. Fun way to cool off on hot summer days.

Splash Features

Interactive water features and splash areas for younger children.

Lap Lanes

Designated lanes for lap swimming during certain hours.

Shallow Area

Shallow water areas for younger swimmers and those learning to swim.

Visiting

Season

Open Memorial Day through Labor Day. Weather-dependent closures possible.

Hours

Hours vary throughout the season. Check city website for current schedule.

Admission

Daily admission fees apply. Resident discounts available. Season passes for frequent visitors.

Programs

Swim Lessons

Summer swim lessons for children. Various skill levels offered.

Pool Parties

Private pool rentals available for birthday parties and events.

Tips

Arrive early on hot days. Pool can reach capacity on the hottest summer days.

Bring sunscreen. Colorado sun is intense. Reapply frequently.

Pack snacks. Concessions available but bringing food saves money.

Check weather. Pool may close for lightning or inclement weather.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the pool open?

Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day. Check for specific hours.

How much does it cost?

Daily admission fees vary. Residents receive discounted rates.

Are there swim lessons?

Yes—summer swim lessons available. Register in advance.

Can I bring food?

Outside food policies vary. Concessions available on-site.

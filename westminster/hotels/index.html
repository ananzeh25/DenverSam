Westminster Hotels

Quick Info

Location: Between Denver and Boulder. Access: US-36, I-25, near DIA. Types: Business hotels, family-friendly, extended stay. Best for: Visiting both Denver and Boulder. Parking: Usually free.

Westminster's location between Denver and Boulder makes it a strategic base for exploring both cities. Hotels cluster near major highways with easy access to attractions in either direction.

Hotel Areas

US-36 Corridor

Hotels near Westminster Promenade and US-36. Good for Boulder-bound travelers.

Near The Orchard

Hotels near I-25 and 144th Avenue. Walking distance to dining and shopping.

Hotel Types

Full-Service

Westin Westminster and similar properties with restaurants, pools, and meeting facilities.

Select-Service

Hampton Inn, Courtyard, and similar brands. Reliable comfort with essential amenities.

Extended Stay

Residence Inn and extended-stay properties for longer visits.

Why Stay in Westminster

Central Location

Between Denver (15 min) and Boulder (20 min). Good base for exploring both.

Value

Often lower rates than downtown Denver with free parking.

Butterfly Pavilion

Stay near the main Westminster attraction for easy morning visits.

Tips

US-36 express lanes. Make Boulder commutes predictable.

Free parking. Most Westminster hotels include it.

Check Broomfield. Adjacent city has additional options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far from Denver?

About 15 miles from downtown Denver via I-25 or US-36.

How far from DIA?

About 25-30 minutes via I-25 or E-470.

Is there a nice hotel?

Westin Westminster is the upscale option. Good meeting facilities and amenities.

Good for families?

Yes—near Butterfly Pavilion and Standley Lake. Suburban, family-friendly area.

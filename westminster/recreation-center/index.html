Westminster Recreation Centers

Quick Info

Main facility: City Park Recreation Center. Amenities: Pools, fitness, gymnasium. Programs: Classes, sports, camps. Access: Day passes and memberships. Hours: Early morning to evening.

Westminster operates recreation centers offering pools, fitness equipment, sports courts, and extensive programming. City Park Recreation Center is the primary facility serving residents and visitors.

City Park Recreation Center

Overview

Full-service recreation center at City Park. Indoor and outdoor facilities for year-round use.

Aquatics

Indoor pool for lap swimming, water fitness, and lessons. Outdoor pool open in summer.

Fitness

Cardio equipment, weight machines, free weights, and stretching areas.

Gymnasium

Courts for basketball, volleyball, and other sports. Open gym and organized programs.

Programs

Youth Programs

Swim lessons, youth sports, day camps, and after-school activities.

Adult Programs

Fitness classes, sports leagues, and specialized programs.

Senior Programs

Fitness and social programs for seniors.

Access & Fees

Day Passes

Day passes available for visitors. Good for pools and fitness facilities.

Memberships

Monthly and annual options. Resident and non-resident rates.

Tips

Check schedules. Pool and facility hours vary. Verify before visiting.

Peak times. After work and weekends busiest.

Register early. Popular programs fill quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can non-residents use the facilities?

Yes—day passes and non-resident memberships available at higher rates.

Is there an outdoor pool?

Yes—outdoor pool open during summer season.

How much is a day pass?

Check current rates on Westminster's website. Varies by resident status.

The Orchard Town Center

Quick Info

Type: Open-air shopping center. Features: Restaurants, retail, entertainment. Anchors: JCPenney, various national retailers. Location: 144th Ave and I-25. Parking: Free. Best for: Dining, shopping, movies.

The Orchard Town Center is Westminster's primary shopping and dining destination. The open-air layout creates a walkable town-center atmosphere with restaurants, stores, and entertainment venues.

Dining

Sit-Down Restaurants

Full-service restaurants with diverse cuisines. Good options for family dinners, date nights, and business lunches.

Fast Casual

Quick-service restaurants for lunch and grab-and-go meals.

Coffee & Dessert

Coffee shops and dessert spots throughout the center.

Shopping

Anchor Stores

JCPenney and other major retailers anchor the center.

Specialty Retail

Mix of national chains and specialty stores. Clothing, home goods, sporting goods, and more.

Services

Banks, salons, and professional services round out the tenant mix.

Entertainment

Movie Theater

Multi-screen cinema showing first-run films. Good option for dinner-and-movie outings.

Events

Seasonal events and community activities throughout the year.

Location & Access

By Car

Located at 144th Avenue and I-25. Easy freeway access from anywhere in the north metro.

Parking

Free parking throughout the center.

Tips

Weekday dining. Restaurants less crowded on weekday evenings.

Combine activities. Shopping, dining, and movies in one walkable destination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a mall in Westminster?

The Orchard is an open-air center, not an enclosed mall. Westminster Promenade is another nearby option.

Is parking free?

Yes—free parking throughout.

What restaurants are there?

Mix of casual dining chains and local options. Good variety for different tastes.

How does it compare to Flatiron Crossing?

Smaller and more casual. Flatiron has more high-end options; The Orchard is convenient for north Westminster.

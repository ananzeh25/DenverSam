Getting to Westminster

Quick Info

From Denver: 15-20 min via I-25 or US-36. From Boulder: 15-20 min via US-36. From DIA: 25-30 min. Transit: RTD Flatiron Flyer (FF) buses. Key highways: US-36, I-25, 120th Ave.

Westminster sits at the crossroads of I-25 and US-36, making it easily accessible from Denver, Boulder, and the airport. The city spans between these major corridors with multiple access points.

From Downtown Denver

Via I-25

I-25 north to 120th Avenue or 104th Avenue exits. About 15-20 minutes without traffic.

Via US-36

US-36 northwest from downtown. Exit at Sheridan, Federal, or Church Ranch. About 15-20 minutes.

By Bus

RTD Flatiron Flyer (FF) buses run along US-36 corridor. Westminster Station provides connections.

From Boulder

By Car

US-36 east about 15 miles. Exit at Westminster destinations. About 15-20 minutes.

Express Lanes

US-36 express lanes available for a toll. Faster and more predictable during rush hour.

From Denver Airport (DIA)

By Car

Peña Boulevard to I-25 north or E-470 to US-36. About 25-30 minutes.

By Transit

A Line to Union Station, transfer to FF buses on US-36. About 60-75 minutes.

Key Routes

US-36

Major corridor connecting Denver and Boulder through Westminster. Express lanes available.

I-25

North-south freeway on Westminster's eastern edge. Connects to Thornton and Denver.

120th Avenue

Major east-west arterial through northern Westminster.

Tips

US-36 express lanes. Worth considering during rush hour for predictable travel times.

Rush hour. Both I-25 and US-36 congest during commute hours.

Flatiron Flyer. Good bus option for Boulder-Westminster-Denver trips.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a car?

Helpful for exploring Westminster. Bus service connects major points but a car is more convenient.

Is there Uber/Lyft?

Yes—rideshare services operate throughout Westminster.

How are the express lanes?

US-36 express lanes are tolled but significantly faster during rush hours.

Is there light rail?

Not directly—Westminster Station is a bus station on US-36, not light rail.

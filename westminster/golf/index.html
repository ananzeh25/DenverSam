Legacy Ridge Golf Course

Quick Info

Type: Championship municipal course. Holes: 18. Par: 72. Rating: Consistently top-rated in Colorado. Views: Front Range panorama. Facilities: Driving range, pro shop, restaurant. Booking: Online tee times.

Legacy Ridge is Westminster's premier municipal golf course—consistently rated among the best public courses in Colorado. The Arthur Hills-designed layout offers challenging golf with stunning mountain views.

The Course

Layout

18 holes winding through rolling terrain with wetlands, bunkers, and elevation changes. Strategic design rewards thoughtful play.

Views

Front Range mountain panorama visible from many holes. One of the more scenic public courses in the metro area.

Conditions

Well-maintained greens and fairways. Course conditions typically excellent during peak season.

Difficulty

Championship caliber but playable for recreational golfers. Multiple tee boxes accommodate various skill levels.

Facilities

Driving Range

Full practice facility for warming up or working on your game.

Pro Shop

Equipment, apparel, and accessories. Club rental available.

Restaurant

Clubhouse dining with course views. Good for post-round meals.

Lessons

Golf instruction available through the pro shop.

Booking & Rates

Tee Times

Book online through the course website. Advance booking recommended, especially for weekends.

Rates

Municipal rates—more affordable than resort courses. Resident and non-resident pricing available.

Tips

Book early. Popular course fills up. Reserve weekend times well in advance.

Play twilight. Afternoon rates cheaper and summer evenings are beautiful.

Altitude plays. Ball travels farther at altitude—club down on approaches.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Legacy Ridge worth playing?

Yes—one of the better public courses in the Denver area. Worth seeking out for quality municipal golf.

How much does it cost?

Varies by time and day. Typically $50-80 for 18 holes including cart.

Can I walk?

Yes—walking permitted. Some prefer carts due to terrain.

Are there other courses nearby?

Hyland Hills and The Heritage are nearby Westminster/Broomfield options.

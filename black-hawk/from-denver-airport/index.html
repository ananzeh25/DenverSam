Denver Airport to Black Hawk

DIA to Colorado's Casino Capital

Quick Facts: DIA to Black Hawk

An easy casino getaway for out-of-state visitors

Distance: 55 miles via I-70 and Highway 119

Drive Time: 1 hour to 1.5 hours

Route: I-70 west to Exit 244, Highway 6 to Highway 119 through Clear Creek Canyon

Transportation Options

Getting from DIA to Black Hawk

Private Car Service ($180-280)

The most convenient option for out-of-state visitors arriving at DIA. Book in advance for meet-and-greet service.

  • Direct from DIA to your Black Hawk casino
  • Professional driver, no navigation worries
  • About 1-1.5 hours depending on traffic
  • Can arrange round-trip or one-way
  • Book return separately based on your gaming schedule

Uber/Lyft ($80-120)

Available from DIA, but has limitations for Black Hawk trips.

  • Pick up at Level 5, Island 6
  • About 1 hour to Black Hawk
  • Good for getting there
  • Challenge: Getting a ride back from Black Hawk can be difficult, especially late night

Limo Service ($350-500)

For a VIP casino experience, book a limo or party bus from DIA.

  • Meet at baggage claim or curbside
  • Drinks and amenities included
  • Round-trip with wait time
  • Perfect for bachelor/bachelorette parties
  • Groups can split cost effectively

Rental Car ($60-100/day)

Best for multi-day casino trips or if you want to explore Colorado beyond Black Hawk.

  • Take the train from DIA to the Rental Car Center
  • 1-hour drive to Black Hawk
  • Free parking at all casinos
  • Scenic route through Clear Creek Canyon
  • Flexibility to visit Central City, Idaho Springs, or other mountain towns

Budget Option: RTD + Casino Shuttle

The most affordable route requires connecting through Denver:

  1. Take RTD A Line from DIA to Union Station ($10.50, 37 min)
  2. From Denver, catch a casino shuttle to Black Hawk (free-$15)

Note: This requires timing your arrival with shuttle schedules. Check casino shuttle departures in advance and allow buffer time.

Planning Your Casino Trip

Tips for out-of-state visitors

Stay at a Casino Resort

If you're flying in specifically for gaming, consider staying at a Black Hawk casino hotel:

  • Monarch Casino Resort Spa – Full resort, spa, multiple restaurants
  • Ameristar Casino Black Hawk – Large property, hotel rooms
  • The Lodge Casino – Comfortable rooms, good value

Staying overnight eliminates round-trip transportation concerns and lets you enjoy gaming without watching the clock.

Round-Trip Planning

If doing a day trip from DIA, book your return transportation in advance:

  • Private car services can schedule pickup times
  • Uber/Lyft from Black Hawk is unreliable—have a backup plan
  • If driving, remember the mountain road and don't drink excessively

Altitude Reminder

Black Hawk sits at 8,042 feet—higher than Denver. If you're arriving from sea level:

  • Stay hydrated
  • Alcohol affects you more strongly at altitude
  • Take it easy on your first day

Frequently Asked Questions

DIA to Black Hawk transportation

Is there a shuttle from Denver Airport to Black Hawk?

There's no direct shared shuttle from DIA to Black Hawk like there is for ski resorts. Your best options are private car service, Uber/Lyft, rental car, or taking the train to Denver and connecting with a casino shuttle.

How long does it take to get from DIA to Black Hawk?

About 1 to 1.5 hours depending on traffic. The route goes through Denver on I-70 west, then through Clear Creek Canyon on Highway 119.

How much does Uber cost from Denver Airport to Black Hawk?

Typically $80-120 depending on demand. The challenge is getting a ride back—Uber availability in Black Hawk is limited, especially late at night.

Should I rent a car to go to Black Hawk from DIA?

A rental car makes sense for multi-day trips or if you want to explore beyond Black Hawk. For a quick casino trip, private car service is more convenient. Remember to have a designated driver if you plan to drink.

Can I fly into Denver just for a day of gambling?

Yes, but consider timing carefully. You'll need 1+ hour each way for transportation. A same-day trip works if you arrive early morning and depart late evening. Staying overnight at a casino hotel is more relaxed.

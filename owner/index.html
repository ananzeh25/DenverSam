Abe's Favorite Spots in Denver

When Abe isn't on the road, these are his top recommended destinations in Denver — tried, tested, and local-approved.

Red Rocks Amphitheatre

Live Music & Hiking

One of the most iconic venues in the world, carved into ancient red sandstone formations 15 miles west of Denver. Go for a show, or hike the trails early morning when the stage is yours.

"I stop here on my way back from a western haul — nothing beats pulling up to those rocks after a long day on I-70."

Recommended Destination

Union Station

Food, Drinks & Culture

Denver's beautifully restored 1914 train station turned social hub. Great restaurants, craft cocktails, and a lively atmosphere right in the heart of LoDo — day or night.

"My go-to spot to decompress after a long dispatch week. The Terminal Bar never misses."

Recommended Destination

RiNo Art District

Art, Breweries & Street Culture

River North's creative corridor is packed with world-class murals, craft breweries, galleries, and some of Denver's best restaurants. Walk, explore, and find something new every visit.

"The murals out here are unreal. I walk through RiNo when I need to clear my head between loads."

Recommended Destination

City Park

Nature & Family

Denver's largest park is home to the Denver Zoo and the Museum of Nature & Science. Bring a picnic, rent a paddleboat on Ferril Lake, and catch mountain views on a clear day.

"Best free view of the Rockies in the city. I bring family here every time they visit from out of state."

Recommended Destination

Larimer Square

Dining & Nightlife

Denver's oldest and most beloved block — lined with Victorian-era buildings, string lights, award-winning restaurants, and boutique shops. The place to go for a memorable night out.

"First place I take anyone new to Denver. The string lights at night make the whole block feel different."

Recommended Destination

Denver Botanic Gardens

Nature & Relaxation

A hidden gem in the middle of the city — 24 acres of stunning curated gardens, seasonal blooms, and summer concerts. A peaceful escape that locals love year-round.

"Most people don't know about this place. Quiet, beautiful, and five minutes from downtown — perfect reset spot."

Recommended Destination

Island Grove Regional Park

Quick Info

Location: Central Greeley. Size: 150+ acres. Features: Events center, Stampede grounds, pool, ballfields. Home to: Greeley Stampede, Centennial Village.

Island Grove Regional Park is Greeley's primary outdoor recreation and events facility. The 150+ acre park hosts the annual Greeley Stampede, houses Centennial Village Museum, and provides year-round recreation for residents and visitors.

Park Facilities

Events Center: Indoor arena and exhibition halls host concerts, trade shows, livestock events, and community gatherings. The facility anchors the Stampede and hosts events year-round.

Outdoor Arena: The rodeo arena comes alive during Stampede but also hosts other equestrian and outdoor events.

Family FunPlex: Water park with pools, slides, and splash areas. Open summers - a popular spot for families beating the heat.

Sports Fields: Baseball and softball diamonds, soccer fields, and open space for recreation.

Centennial Village: The outdoor history museum occupies a section of the park with 35+ historic buildings.

Stampede Grounds

During the Greeley Stampede (late June through July 4th), the park transforms into rodeo and carnival central. The midway, concert stages, vendor areas, and rodeo facilities all operate within Island Grove.

Outside Stampede season, the grounds are quieter but still host various events and provide open space for recreation.

Visiting the Park

Access: The park is free to enter except during special events. Parking is available throughout.

FunPlex: The water park charges admission and operates seasonally (summer months).

Events: Check the Island Grove event calendar for what's happening during your visit. The events center hosts something most weekends.

Walking/Jogging: Paths wind through the park for casual recreation. It's flat and accessible.

What's Nearby

Island Grove's central location makes it easy to combine with other Greeley activities:

  • Centennial Village - within the park
  • Downtown Greeley - short drive for food and drinks
  • Breweries - post-visit refreshment

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the park free?

General park access is free. The FunPlex and special events charge admission.

When is the FunPlex open?

Summer months only, typically Memorial Day through Labor Day. Check current hours and admission.

Can I visit the rodeo grounds when there's no Stampede?

The grounds are accessible but mostly empty outside of events. Not much to see without an event happening.

Is there food in the park?

During events, yes. Otherwise, head to downtown Greeley for dining options.

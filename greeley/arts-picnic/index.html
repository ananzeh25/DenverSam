Greeley Arts Picnic

Quick Info

When: Typically late July/early August weekend. Where: Lincoln Park, downtown Greeley. Admission: Free. Features: Juried art show, food vendors, live music, kids' activities.

The Greeley Arts Picnic is an annual summer arts festival held in downtown Greeley's Lincoln Park. The event showcases regional artists, offers live entertainment, and brings the community together for a weekend of creativity and celebration.

Running for over 50 years, the Arts Picnic is a beloved local tradition - smaller and more intimate than major Colorado festivals, with a genuine community feel.

The Art

The juried art show is the centerpiece. Artists from around the region display and sell work including:

  • Paintings and prints
  • Pottery and ceramics
  • Jewelry
  • Sculpture
  • Photography
  • Mixed media and crafts

Work ranges from affordable pieces to significant investments. The juried format ensures quality. Artists are typically present to discuss their work.

Entertainment

Live music on multiple stages throughout the weekend. Local and regional acts perform a variety of genres. The park setting creates a relaxed atmosphere for enjoying performances.

Beyond music, you may find:

  • Dance performances
  • Art demonstrations
  • Interactive activities
  • Street performers

Food and Drink

Food vendors set up throughout the park. Expect festival fare along with local favorites. Downtown restaurants are also within walking distance if you want to escape the crowds for a sit-down meal.

Beer gardens typically operate within the festival grounds.

Kids' Activities

The Arts Picnic includes programming for children:

  • Art-making activities
  • Interactive displays
  • Entertainment geared toward families

It's a family-friendly event with plenty for younger visitors to enjoy.

Attending the Festival

Admission: Free to attend. You pay for food, drinks, and any art you purchase.

Parking: Downtown parking fills up. Arrive early or be prepared to walk from remote areas.

Weather: Late summer in Greeley can be hot. Bring sun protection. Afternoon thunderstorms are possible.

Timing: Mornings are less crowded. Late afternoon and evening bring the largest crowds and best entertainment.

Frequently Asked Questions

When exactly is the Arts Picnic?

Usually a weekend in late July or early August. Check the Greeley events calendar for specific dates.

How big is it?

A solid regional festival - larger than small-town fairs but more intimate than Cherry Creek Arts Festival in Denver.

Can I bring a blanket or chairs?

Yes, bring seating for enjoying music. Check current policies for any restrictions.

Are dogs allowed?

Policies vary by year. Well-behaved leashed dogs are often permitted, but check current rules.

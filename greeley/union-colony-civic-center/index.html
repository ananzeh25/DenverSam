Union Colony Civic Center

Quick Info

What: Performing arts and events center. Capacity: ~1,700 seats (main hall). Events: Concerts, theater, comedy, community events. Location: Downtown Greeley. Named for: Greeley's founding community.

The Union Colony Civic Center is Greeley's premier performing arts venue, hosting touring concerts, theatrical productions, comedy shows, and community events. Located in downtown Greeley, the center brings cultural programming to northern Colorado without requiring the drive to Denver.

What's On Stage

Concerts: Touring musicians across genres - country, rock, folk, tribute bands, and more. The 1,700-seat main hall provides good acoustics for live music.

Theater: Broadway touring productions, regional theater companies, and local productions. The venue hosts family shows, musicals, and dramatic works.

Comedy: Stand-up comedians on the touring circuit stop in Greeley. Shows range from nationally known headliners to up-and-coming acts.

Community events: Graduations, award ceremonies, lectures, and other community gatherings use the space.

The Venues

Monfort Concert Hall: The main performance space seating approximately 1,700. Good sightlines and acoustics for concerts and large productions.

Hensel Phelps Theatre: Smaller, more intimate space for theater productions, lectures, and smaller concerts.

Both venues are well-maintained and provide professional-quality settings for performances.

Attending an Event

Tickets: Available through the civic center box office and online. Prices vary by event - touring Broadway shows cost more than local productions.

Parking: Downtown parking available nearby. Arrive early for popular shows.

Dining: Downtown Greeley restaurants are walking distance for pre-show dinner.

Dress: Casual to dressy depending on the event. Greeley audiences tend toward relaxed attire.

Why It Matters

The civic center brings cultural programming to Greeley that might otherwise require a trip to Denver. For residents of northern Colorado, it provides access to touring acts and professional productions closer to home. For visitors, it can anchor an evening in Greeley with dinner and a show.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I see what's playing?

Check the Union Colony Civic Center website for the current season schedule and tickets.

Is it comparable to Denver venues?

It's smaller than major Denver venues but well-appointed. The intimate size can be an advantage for some shows.

Are there student discounts?

Some shows offer student pricing. Check individual event listings.

What's the best seating?

Orchestra center is premium. Balcony seats offer good views at lower prices for most shows.

Dinosaur Journey Museum

Quick Info

What: Dinosaur museum with locally-discovered fossils. Highlights: Moving dinosaur robots, working paleontology lab. Good for: Families with kids. Time needed: 1-2 hours. Location: Downtown Fruita.

Dinosaur Journey showcases the paleontological heritage of the Grand Valley, where significant dinosaur discoveries continue to be made. The museum features fossils found in the surrounding desert, animatronic dinosaurs, and hands-on exhibits that engage visitors of all ages.

Exhibits

Local Dinosaurs

See fossils discovered in the Fruita area, including specimens found at local dig sites. The region has produced important finds from the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods.

Animated Dinosaurs

Life-size robotic dinosaurs move and roar throughout the museum. Kids love them; adults appreciate the scientific accuracy.

Working Lab

Watch paleontologists prepare real fossils through the lab window. Staff sometimes answer questions about ongoing work.

Hands-On Activities

Touch real fossils, dig in the sandbox for buried "bones," and engage with interactive displays. Excellent for young dinosaur enthusiasts.

Visiting

Hours: Open daily. Check current hours online.

Admission: Modest fee. Discounts for children and seniors.

Duration: Most visitors spend 1-2 hours.

Tips

Great rainy day option. If weather disrupts biking plans, this fills time well.

Kids love it. The animatronics and hands-on activities keep children engaged.

Combine with other Fruita attractions. Quick stop between mountain biking and Colorado National Monument.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dinosaur Journey worth visiting without kids?

The science is legitimate and the local fossil connection is interesting. Worth a quick visit for anyone curious about paleontology.

Can I join a dig?

The museum sometimes offers dig programs. Check their website for current offerings.

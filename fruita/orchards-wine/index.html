Grand Valley Orchards & Wine

Quick Info

What: Colorado wine country and fruit orchards. Famous for: Peaches, wine grapes, apples. Best time: Late summer for peaches, year-round for wine tasting. Location: Palisade area, 15-20 minutes from Fruita.

The Grand Valley's warm climate and irrigation make it Colorado's premier fruit-growing region. Palisade peaches are legendary throughout the state, and the area has developed into Colorado's largest wine-producing region with dozens of wineries and tasting rooms.

Wine Country

Colorado Wine

The Grand Valley's high desert climate produces distinctive wines. Varietals include Merlot, Cabernet, Riesling, and Viognier. Tasting rooms welcome visitors throughout the year.

Wine Tasting

Dozens of wineries offer tastings. Many cluster around Palisade, making self-guided tours easy. Some offer tours of vineyards and production facilities.

Notable Wineries

Established producers include Two Rivers Winery, Carlson Vineyards, and Plum Creek Winery. Each has its own character and specialties.

Fruit & Orchards

Palisade Peaches

The region's signature crop. Peak season is late July through August. Farm stands sell fresh peaches—far superior to supermarket versions.

Other Fruits

Apples, cherries, apricots, and pears also grow in the valley. Different fruits ripen throughout summer and fall.

Farm Stands

Roadside stands sell fresh fruit in season. Cash is often preferred. Best selection is mornings before crowds.

Tips

Peach season is August. Plan a visit for peak Palisade peach season if that's your goal.

Wine tasting is year-round. A nice change of pace from mountain biking.

Designated driver needed. Multiple tasting stops require planning for safe transport.

Combine with other activities. Wine and peaches complement a Fruita biking or Colorado National Monument visit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Colorado wine actually good?

The Grand Valley produces legitimate wine. Quality has improved significantly. Worth exploring with an open mind.

When are Palisade peaches available?

Late July through August is peak season. Timing varies by year. Farm stands have the freshest selection.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our commitment to flexibility and fairness

Our Commitment to You: At Denver Sam, we understand plans can change. Our flexible refund policy is designed to be fair to both our valued customers and our dedicated drivers.

1. Flexible Refund Structure

  • 24+ hours notice: 100% refund
  • 12-24 hours notice: 90% refund
  • 6-12 hours notice: 75% refund
  • 3-6 hours notice: 50% refund or 100% credit toward future ride
  • Less than 3 hours: Non-refundable

2. Flexible Options

Instead of a refund, customers may choose:

  • 100% credit toward any future booking (valid for 12 months)
  • Rescheduling to any available time slot within 30 days
  • Transfer the booking to another person

3. No-Questions-Asked Grace Period

All bookings include a 30-minute grace period after confirmation during which you can cancel for any reason with a full refund.

4. Easy Cancellation Process

Cancel through any of these convenient methods:

  • Email to info@denversam.com

5. Fast Refund Processing

  • Immediate processing for cancellations made 6+ hours in advance
  • Within 3 business days for all other eligible refunds
  • Instant credits available for future bookings

6. Our Driver Protection Promise

While we prioritize customer flexibility, cancellation fees help us fairly compensate drivers who have already begun their journey to serve you.

7. Contact Us

Questions? Concerns? Special circumstances? We're here to help!

  • Email: info@denversam.com
  • Website: www.denversam.com

Our customer service team has the flexibility to make exceptions when needed.

Your satisfaction is our priority. Thank you for choosing Denver Sam!

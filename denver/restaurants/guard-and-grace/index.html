Guard and Grace

Denver's Premier Steakhouse

Guard and Grace is where Denver does special occasions right. Prime steaks, an impressive seafood tower, and a rooftop bar that rivals any in the city. It's expensive, but the quality justifies the price tag. Book ahead for weekends.

What to Order

Steaks: The bone-in ribeye and filet are exceptional. Wagyu options available for those who want to splurge harder. All steaks come with compound butter - ask for extra.

Seafood Tower: A showstopper for groups. Oysters, shrimp, crab, lobster on ice. Start with this while you wait for steaks.

Sides: The creamed corn is famous. Brussels sprouts with bacon deliver. Order family-style for the table.

The Rooftop

Guard and Grace's rooftop bar is a destination in itself. Downtown views, craft cocktails, lighter bites if you're not doing a full steakhouse dinner. Arrive early for sunset drinks before your reservation.

Pro Tip: Happy hour (4-6pm Mon-Fri) offers discounted apps and drinks at the bar. A great way to experience Guard and Grace without the full steakhouse price tag.

Pricing

Plan for $80-150 per person with drinks. Steaks range from $55-165 depending on cut and grade. The seafood tower starts at $95. It's expensive, but the execution matches the price.

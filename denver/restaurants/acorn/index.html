What to Order

Wood-Fired Dishes: Everything from the oak-burning oven. The blistered peppers and wood-roasted cauliflower are signatures.

Order 4-5 plates per couple and share. The menu is designed for exploration.

Cocktails: The bar program is excellent. Start with a drink while you browse the menu.

Pro Tip: Explore The Source before or after dinner. The food hall has Crooked Stave taproom, a butcher, bakery, and more under one roof.

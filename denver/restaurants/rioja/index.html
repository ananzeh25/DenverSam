Rioja

Mediterranean Elegance on Larimer Square

Rioja is where Denver's fine dining scene began its modern era. James Beard Award-winning chef Jennifer Jasinski has run this Larimer Square gem since 2004, serving Mediterranean cuisine that's both refined and welcoming. For a special occasion in Denver, this is the benchmark.

What to Order

Artisan Cheese: The cheese course is legendary. Colorado and European selections with house-made accompaniments.

Pasta: The handmade pastas change seasonally. Whatever's on the menu, order it.

Seasonal Tasting: Let the kitchen guide you with the chef's tasting menu for the full experience.

The Experience

Sophisticated but not stuffy. White tablecloths, attentive service, and a wine list that goes deep into Spanish and Mediterranean regions. The bar is excellent for a pre-dinner drink or a lighter meal.

Pro Tip: Request a table by the window for Larimer Square views. After dinner, walk the square's string lights - it's Denver's most romantic block.

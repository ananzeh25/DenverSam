Work & Class

Blue-Collar Food, White-Tablecloth Quality

Work & Class serves hearty Southern and Latin-inspired dishes from a wood-fired oven in RiNo. The portions are generous, prices are fair, and the industrial-chic space fills up fast. This is the kind of place where you leave full and happy without breaking the bank.

What to Order

Pork Green Chile: A Colorado classic done right. Rich, hearty, and served with fresh tortillas.

Smoked Meats: The wood-fired oven produces excellent brisket, ribs, and pulled pork. Get a meat board to share.

Cornbread: Don't skip it. Comes with honey butter that's worth every calorie.

The Vibe

Industrial RiNo space with exposed brick, communal tables, and an open kitchen. It's loud, lively, and unpretentious. The kind of place where you're encouraged to eat with your hands.

Pro Tip: The happy hour (5-6pm daily) has great deals on tacos and drinks. Get there right at 5 for the best shot at bar seats.

Tavernetta

Denver's Best Italian Restaurant

Tavernetta brings northern Italian cuisine to Denver's Union Station neighborhood. Handmade pasta, an impressive Italian wine list, and a stunning two-story space make this the city's top choice for a special Italian meal. Book well ahead for weekends.

What to Order

Pasta: Made fresh daily. The cacio e pepe and handkerchief pasta with lamb ragu are standouts. Don't skip pasta even if you're getting a main.

Antipasti: Start with burrata or beef carpaccio. The bread service with olive oil is excellent.

Wine: Deep Italian list. Let the sommelier guide you - they know the cellar well.

The Space

Two-story restaurant with soaring ceilings, open kitchen, and a bar scene that rivals the dining room. The upstairs mezzanine offers semi-private dining for groups.

Pro Tip: Can't get a dinner reservation? The bar serves the full menu and takes walk-ins. Arrive at 5pm for best shot at bar seats.

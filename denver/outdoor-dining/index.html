Top 10 Downtown Denver Restaurants with Outdoor Seating

What are the best outdoor dining spots in downtown Denver? Top picks: Culinary Dropout (comfort food, great patio), 54thirty Rooftop (best views), Avanti Food & Beverage (food hall with rooftop), Postino (bruschetta and wine), and Joy Hill (wood-fired pizza with rooftop). All feature patios perfect for Denver's 300+ days of sunshine.

Denver's vibrant downtown boasts a diverse culinary scene, with many restaurants offering delightful outdoor seating.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best rooftop restaurant in Denver?

54thirty Rooftop on top of Le Meridien hotel is the highest open-air rooftop bar in Denver with stunning mountain views. Joy Hill also offers a great rooftop patio experience with wood-fired pizzas. Both are best during sunset.

Do Denver restaurants have heated patios in winter?

Yes, many Denver restaurants keep patios open year-round with heaters, fire pits, and blankets. Culinary Dropout, Avanti, and several others offer heated outdoor seating. Denver's sunny winters make patio dining possible even in cold months.

Is Avanti Food & Beverage worth visiting?

Yes, Avanti is a popular food hall with multiple restaurant concepts under one roof, plus a rooftop patio with city views. Great for groups who can't agree on one cuisine - options include Mexican, burgers, and more. Over 6,000 reviews averaging 4.3 stars.

What's a good outdoor restaurant near Union Station?

Room for Milly at 1615 Platte St is a short walk from Union Station with creative cocktails and small plates on their outdoor patio. Postino Broadway and Culinary Dropout are also within easy reach of downtown.

Do I need reservations for outdoor dining in Denver?

For rooftop spots like 54thirty and popular patios like Culinary Dropout, reservations are strongly recommended, especially for weekend evenings. Food halls like Avanti are first-come, first-served but can get crowded during peak hours.

What's the best outdoor dining for groups?

Culinary Dropout has a large patio perfect for groups, with shareable plates like Pretzel Bites and 36-Hour Pork Ribs. Avanti Food & Beverage works well for groups since everyone can order from different vendors and meet on the rooftop.

From Denver Airport to Union Station: A Quick Transit Guide

Taking the A Line train from Denver International Airport to Union Station is the most affordable way to reach downtown Denver. At just $10 for a day pass, you'll be at Union Station in 37 minutes while enjoying scenic views of the city skyline and mountains.

Finding the Transit Center

After collecting your luggage on Level 5, head to the south side of the terminal. Follow signs for "Transit Center" or "Trains to City" through Jeppesen Terminal.

Step-by-Step Directions:

  1. Exit baggage claim (Level 5) on the south side
  2. Follow signs for "Transit Center" or "Trains to City"
  3. Take escalator down to Level 1
  4. Look for Denver Airport Station near the Westin Hotel

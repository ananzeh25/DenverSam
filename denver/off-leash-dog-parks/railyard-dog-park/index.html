Railyard Dog Park

Downtown Denver's Dog Spot

Railyard Dog Park gives downtown Denver dog owners a nearby off-leash option. Located in the Commons Park area near Confluence Park, it's the go-to spot for LoHi, Platte, and downtown residents who don't want to drive to the suburbs.

Urban Dog Park Life

Size: Smaller urban park - enough for a quick play session but not for marathon fetch games.

Crowd: Popular with apartment dwellers. Can get busy during morning and evening rush hours.

Location: Can't beat the convenience. Walk from your downtown apartment, let the dog run, grab coffee on the way home.

Combine With

After the dog park, take a leashed walk along the South Platte or Cherry Creek trails. Confluence Park is right there for riverside hangouts.

Pro Tip: Street parking is tough in this area. Walk, bike, or take the light rail. The closest station is Union Station.

Berkeley Dog Park

Northwest Denver's Go-To Spot

Berkeley Dog Park is a local favorite in northwest Denver, offering a large fenced area near Berkeley Lake with views of the Front Range. The regular crowd knows each other's dogs, and the vibe is friendly and social.

The Park

Size: Medium-sized fenced area with enough room for dogs to run without feeling crowded.

Surface: Mix of grass and dirt. Can get muddy after rain.

Views: Clear sight of the mountains on good days. One of the more scenic dog parks in the city.

Community

The regulars are friendly and welcoming. Morning and evening crowds are the most consistent - many of the same dogs and owners show up daily.

Pro Tip: After the dog park, walk around Berkeley Lake with your pup. Then hit Tennyson Street for coffee or lunch at one of the dog-friendly patios.

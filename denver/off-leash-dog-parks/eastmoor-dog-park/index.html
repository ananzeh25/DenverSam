Eastmoor Dog Park

Room to Run in Southeast Denver

Eastmoor Park offers a large off-leash area for dogs who need serious room to run. Located in southeast Denver, it's a good choice for high-energy breeds and dogs who don't do well in crowded spaces.

For Active Dogs

Space: One of the larger fenced off-leash areas in southeast Denver. Your dog can actually stretch their legs here.

Fetch: Enough room for long throws. Great for ball-obsessed dogs who need distance.

Lower Traffic: Not as crowded as central Denver parks. More room per dog.

Considerations

Shade: Limited tree coverage. Bring water and avoid peak sun hours in summer.

Surface: Grass and dirt. Can get dusty in dry weather.

Pro Tip: If your dog needs to burn serious energy, this is the place. Bring a frisbee or chuck-it and let them run.

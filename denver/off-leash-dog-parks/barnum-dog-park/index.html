Barnum Dog Park

West Denver's Friendly Spot

Barnum Dog Park serves west Denver with a well-maintained fenced area that includes good shade and a separate section for small dogs. The neighborhood vibe is friendly, and the park is clean and well-used.

What Makes It Work

Shade: Mature trees provide real shade - a rarity in Denver dog parks. Summer visits are comfortable.

Small Dog Area: Separate fenced section for dogs under 25 pounds. Peace of mind for small dog owners.

Community: The neighborhood regulars are welcoming. Good mix of dogs and owners.

Pro Tip: The broader Barnum Park has trails, a lake, and picnic areas. Make it a full outing with your dog.

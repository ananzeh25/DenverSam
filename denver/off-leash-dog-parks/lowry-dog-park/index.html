Lowry Dog Park

East Denver's Top Choice

Lowry Dog Park is a well-maintained off-leash area in the Lowry neighborhood, featuring separate spaces for large and small dogs. The open grassland gives dogs plenty of room, and the community of regulars keeps things friendly.

Why Dog Owners Love It

Small Dog Area: A separate fenced section for dogs under 25 pounds. Small dog owners appreciate not having to worry about bigger dogs.

Clean & Maintained: Well-kept by the city and regulars. Grass is in good shape, and there's water for dogs.

Community: Morning and evening regulars are welcoming. Good place to meet other dog owners in the neighborhood.

Pro Tip: The Lowry neighborhood has great coffee shops and restaurants nearby. Many have dog-friendly patios for post-park refreshments.

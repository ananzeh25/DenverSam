Dakota Grange Dog Park

Central Park's Modern Spot

Dakota Grange is one of Denver's newer dog parks, built to serve the growing Central Park neighborhood. Modern design, excellent facilities, and plenty of space make it one of the best-designed parks in the city.

Modern Design

Built Right: Newer park means better design. Proper drainage, quality fencing, and thoughtful layout.

Facilities: Water stations that work, seating for owners, and some shade structures.

Space: Generous size for the neighborhood. Room for dogs to run without constant crowding.

The Neighborhood

Central Park (formerly Stapleton) is one of Denver's fastest-growing areas. The dog park serves a young, active community of dog owners.

Pro Tip: The area has great coffee shops and restaurants. Grab a drink after the park at one of the nearby spots with outdoor seating.

Best Off-Leash Dog Parks in Denver

Let Your Pup Run Free

Denver is one of the most dog-friendly cities in America, with over 15 official off-leash dog parks scattered across neighborhoods. From huge open spaces to fenced urban parks, here are the best spots to let your pup run free.

Cherry Creek Dog Park

Denver's Largest

107 acres of off-leash heaven in Cherry Creek State Park. Trails, pond access, and room to run.

107 acres Water Access $11 Entry

Berkeley Dog Park

Northwest Denver Favorite

Large fenced area near Berkeley Lake with mountain views. Popular with local dogs and owners.

Fenced Lake Nearby Free

Lowry Dog Park

East Denver's Best

Open grassland in the Lowry neighborhood. Separate areas for large and small dogs.

Fenced Small Dog Area Free

Green Valley Ranch

Northeast Denver

Large open space with prairie views. Less crowded than central Denver parks.

Spacious Less Crowded Free

Kennedy Dog Park

Globeville Gem

Hidden north Denver park with excellent facilities. Great community of regular dog owners.

Fenced Water Station Free

Railyard Dog Park

LoHi / Platte

Urban dog park near Confluence Park. Great for downtown residents and their pups.

Fenced Downtown Free

Barnum Dog Park

West Denver

West Denver park with good shade and separate small dog area. Friendly neighborhood vibe.

Fenced Small Dog Area Shaded

Congress Park

Central Denver

Small but popular park in a great central location. Regulars know each other's dogs by name.

Fenced Central Free

Dakota Grange

Central Park

Newer park in Central Park neighborhood. Well-maintained with great facilities.

Fenced Modern Free

Eastmoor Park

Southeast Denver

Large off-leash area in southeast Denver. Good for high-energy dogs who need room.

Large Open Space Free

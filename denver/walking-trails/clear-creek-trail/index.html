Clear Creek Trail

Golden to Downtown Denver

The Clear Creek Trail connects downtown Golden to the South Platte River Trail in Denver - over 20 miles of paved path following a beautiful mountain creek. Start in Golden with a Coors Brewery tour, then ride or walk all the way to downtown Denver.

Trail Highlights

Golden Section: Start at the Coors Brewery or downtown Golden. The creek rushes through town with kayakers playing in the whitewater park. Cafes and breweries line the route.

Wheat Ridge / Arvada: Suburban stretch with good shade and creek access. Several parks with restrooms along the way.

Denver Connection: Joins the South Platte River Trail near I-76. From there, it's a quick ride to downtown.

Best Uses

Biking: The classic use. Slightly downhill from Golden to Denver makes for an easy ride. Return by bike or take RTD.

Running: Wide, flat, scenic. Some sections get crowded on weekends.

Walking: Pick a segment. The Golden section is most picturesque for a casual walk.

Pro Tip: Start in Golden and ride to Denver. The slight downhill makes the 20 miles feel easier. Take the light rail back (G Line) with your bike.

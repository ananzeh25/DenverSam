Bear Creek Lake Park

Lakewood's Premier Outdoor Destination

Bear Creek Lake Park packs a lot into its 2,600 acres just southwest of Denver. A swim beach, paddleboard rentals, 15 miles of trails, fishing, and archery range. It's the full outdoor experience without driving to the mountains.

Activities

Swim Beach: Sandy beach with lifeguards from Memorial Day to Labor Day. Cleaner and less crowded than Cherry Creek on weekdays.

Paddleboarding & Kayaking: Rent SUPs, kayaks, and pedal boats on-site. Bring your own for free - no motorized boats.

Hiking & Biking: 15 miles of trails ranging from easy lakeside paths to challenging climbs with mountain views.

Fishing: Stocked with trout, bass, and catfish. Fish from shore or non-motorized boats.

More to Do

Archery Range: Free public archery range - bring your own equipment.

Disc Golf: 18-hole course through scenic terrain.

Horse Stables: Equestrian trails and boarding facilities on-site.

Pro Tip: The Mount Carbon trail offers the best views but is a solid climb. Start early on hot days. The lake views from the top are worth it.

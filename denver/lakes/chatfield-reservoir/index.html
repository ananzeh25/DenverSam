Chatfield State Park

Full Recreation Experience Near Denver

Chatfield State Park offers the most complete outdoor recreation experience near Denver. The 1,500-acre reservoir supports boating, waterskiing, and swimming, while the surrounding park provides camping, hiking, horseback riding, and even a hot air balloon port. It's 20 minutes from downtown but feels like a getaway.

On the Water

Boating: Full marina with boat rentals, launch ramps, and fuel. Motorboats, sailboats, and jet skis all welcome.

Swim Beach: Sandy beach with lifeguards from Memorial Day through Labor Day. Picnic areas and concessions nearby.

Fishing: Excellent walleye, bass, and trout fishing. Fish from shore, your own boat, or rent one at the marina.

On Land

Camping: 197 sites including full hookups, tent sites, and group camping. Book ahead for summer weekends.

Trails: 26 miles of multi-use trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding. The Plum Creek Trail connects to a larger trail network.

Hot Air Balloons: The park has a designated balloon port - watch launches in the early morning.

Pro Tip: The south entrance is closest to the swim beach and marina. The north entrance is better for camping and trails. Plan accordingly.

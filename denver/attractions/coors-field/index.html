Coors Field

Colorado Rockies Baseball in Downtown Denver

Coors Field is one of baseball's best stadiums, with mountain views, rooftop seating, and a downtown LoDo location perfect for pre and post-game dining. Even if you're not a Rockies fan, the experience of catching a game here is worth it. The thin mile-high air means more home runs than any other park.

Best Seats

Rockpile: Center field bleachers for $4-6. Best value in baseball. Gets direct sun for day games, but the atmosphere is fun.

Rooftop: The Rooftop deck offers bar seating with mountain views. Great for a casual game experience.

Club Level: Behind home plate with access to indoor/outdoor bars. More upscale, less crazy.

Tips for Game Day

Gates open 90 minutes before first pitch. Get there early for batting practice if you want to catch a ball.

The LoDo bars fill up before and after games. Blake Street is the main strip. Reservations help for popular spots.

Pro Tip: Take the light rail from Union Station - it's one block from the stadium. Skip parking hassles and grab a drink at Union Station before walking over.

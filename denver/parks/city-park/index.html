City Park

Denver's Cultural Heart

City Park is Denver's largest park at 330 acres and home to two of the city's top attractions: the Denver Zoo and the Denver Museum of Nature & Science. Beyond the institutions, it's an urban oasis with stunning mountain views, a lake, and endless green space.

The Attractions

Denver Zoo: One of the country's top zoos. Gorillas, elephants, giraffes, and a popular Toyota Elephant Passage. Plan half a day minimum.

Museum of Nature & Science: World-class natural history museum with an IMAX theater and planetarium. The dinosaur exhibits are exceptional.

The Park Itself

Ferril Lake: The centerpiece. Rent a paddle boat in summer or just walk the perimeter. Mountain views across the water are iconic Denver.

City Park Golf Course: Public 18-hole course with affordable rates and downtown skyline views.

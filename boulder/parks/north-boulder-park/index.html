North Boulder Park: Playground, Tennis & Community Events

What is North Boulder Park? A beloved neighborhood park in the NoBo (North Boulder) area with a playground, tennis courts, open grass, and mountain views. Hosts the North Boulder Farmers Market on Saturdays and summer concerts. More local vibe, less touristy than downtown parks. Free entry.

Overview

Location: Broadway & Quince Ave, North Boulder
Size: 10 acres
Entry Fee: Free
Hours: 5am-11pm

North Boulder Park is the heart of the NoBo neighborhood. While tourists crowd Chautauqua and Central Park, this park stays relaxed and local. Families bring kids to the playground, neighbors walk dogs, and summer weekends bring farmers markets and live music. The Flatirons rise to the west, providing Boulder's signature backdrop.

Features

Playground

Updated playground equipment suitable for various ages. Climbing structures, swings, and a popular rope climbing feature. Rubberized surface. Shaded by mature trees.

Tennis Courts

Four lighted tennis courts, first-come first-served. Popular in evenings and weekends. Bring your own equipment.

Open Space

Large grass areas perfect for picnics, frisbee, soccer, and lounging. The gentle slope provides good views of the mountains. Less manicured than some parks, which adds to the natural feel.

Farmers Market

The North Boulder Farmers Market runs Saturday mornings (8am-1pm) May through October. Smaller and more neighborhood-focused than the Boulder Farmers Market downtown. Local produce, baked goods, food vendors, and live music.

Summer Concerts

Free summer concert series brings live music to the park on select evenings. Families spread blankets on the grass. Check Boulder Parks & Rec for schedules.

Facilities

  • Playground
  • Tennis courts (4, lighted)
  • Restrooms
  • Picnic tables
  • Drinking fountains
  • On-street parking

Nearby

North Boulder Park is walkable to the NoBo neighborhood's coffee shops, restaurants, and breweries along North Broadway. The Lucky's Market complex is nearby for groceries. Walking distance to Ideal Market and the growing NoBo food scene.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the farmers market?

Saturday mornings, 8am-1pm, May through October.

Are dogs allowed?

Yes, on leash. For off-leash play, head to Foothills Dog Park or Valmont Dog Park.

Is there parking?

Street parking around the park is free. It fills during farmers market and events—bike or walk if possible.

Foothills Community Park: Sports, Dog Park & Trails

What is Foothills Community Park? Boulder's largest community park with soccer fields, baseball diamonds, a popular off-leash dog park, skate park, and connections to the Foothills Trail. Located in north Boulder with Flatirons views. Free entry with large parking lots.

Overview

Location: 800 Cherry Ave, Boulder
Size: 83 acres
Entry Fee: Free
Hours: 5am-11pm

Foothills Community Park is Boulder's recreation hub. The sprawling complex hosts youth and adult sports leagues, provides one of the city's best dog parks, and connects to trails that lead south toward Wonderland Lake and the open space system. Less scenic than Chautauqua but more functional for everyday recreation.

Features

Dog Park

The Foothills Dog Park is one of Boulder's most popular off-leash areas. Fenced, with separate sections for small and large dogs. Water stations, shade structures, and benches for owners. Can get crowded on weekend mornings.

Skate Park

Well-designed concrete skate park with bowls, rails, and street elements. Free to use. Popular with skateboarders and BMX riders. Helmets required for riders under 18.

Sports Fields

Multiple soccer fields, baseball diamonds, and multipurpose fields. Most are reserved for league play during peak hours. Open for casual use when not scheduled. The park hosts tournaments throughout the summer.

Community Center

The East Boulder Community Center (adjacent) offers indoor pool, gym, and fitness classes. Membership or day pass required for indoor facilities.

Trail Connections

The Foothills Trail runs along the west side of the park, connecting north to Wonderland Lake and south toward the Boulder Creek Path. Easy, flat walking and biking on paved paths.

Facilities

  • Large free parking lots
  • Public restrooms
  • Drinking fountains
  • Picnic areas
  • Playground
  • Basketball courts
  • Tennis courts

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the dog park fenced?

Yes. The Foothills Dog Park is fully fenced with separate areas for large and small dogs.

Can I use the sports fields for pickup games?

When not reserved for league play, fields are open for public use. Check the schedule at the park.

Is there a fee to use the skate park?

No, the skate park is free and open to the public.

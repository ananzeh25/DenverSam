Boulder Parks

Green spaces at the base of the mountains

Boulder takes its parks seriously. From the iconic Chautauqua meadows to creek-side urban parks, there's green space around every corner. These are the best Boulder parks for picnics, playgrounds, and outdoor relaxation.

Chautauqua Park

Boulder's Crown Jewel

Historic park at the base of the Flatirons. Trails, concerts, dining hall, and the most iconic views in Boulder.

Historic Concerts

Central Park

Downtown Boulder Hub

Downtown gathering space hosting farmers markets, events, and summer concerts. Next to Boulder Creek Path.

Downtown Events

Scott Carpenter Park

Pool, Playground & Rockets

Family-friendly park with outdoor pool, excellent playground, and space-themed elements honoring astronaut Scott Carpenter.

Pool Playground

Eben G. Fine Park

Creek Access & Tubing

Canyon entrance park where tubers put in. Picnic areas, creek access, and the start of Boulder Creek Path.

Tubing Creek Access

North Boulder Park

Sports & Recreation

Large park with sports fields, tennis courts, and playground. Popular with families and athletic leagues.

Sports Tennis

Foothills Community Park

South Boulder Recreation

Large south Boulder park with rec center, baseball fields, and connections to trails. Dog park adjacent.

Rec Center Baseball

Boulder Farmers Market

Colorado's Best Market

The Boulder Farmers Market has been a community institution since 1987. With over 150 vendors selling local produce, prepared foods, baked goods, meats, and crafts, it's widely considered the best farmers market in Colorado. The Saturday market on 13th Street is a Boulder essential.

What to Expect

The Saturday market takes over 13th Street from Arapahoe to Canyon. Expect crowds, live music, cooking demonstrations, and an atmosphere that's part shopping, part social event. Come hungry‚Äîthe prepared food vendors are exceptional.

Vendor Highlights

Produce: Colorado's short growing season means peak freshness. Look for Palisade peaches in August, Olathe sweet corn, and Rocky Ford melons.

Prepared Foods: Thai, Mexican, Nepali, crepes, wood-fired pizza‚Äîthe diversity is remarkable.

Crafts: Local artisans sell pottery, jewelry, soaps, and textiles.

Pro Tip: Arrive early on Saturdays (by 8:30am) for the best selection and smallest crowds. Bring cash‚Äîsome vendors don't take cards. The Wednesday evening market at Central Park is smaller but less crowded and has a relaxed vibe.

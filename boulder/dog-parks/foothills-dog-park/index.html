Foothills Dog Park: Off-Leash Fun Near Trails

Foothills Dog Park at a glance: Boulder's most popular off-leash area within Foothills Community Park. Fully fenced with separate large and small dog areas. Water stations, shade, and Flatirons views. Near the Foothills Trail for leashed walks before or after. Busiest weekend mornings.

Overview

Location: Within Foothills Community Park, 800 Cherry Ave, Boulder
Hours: 5am-11pm
Entry Fee: Free

Foothills Dog Park benefits from its location within Boulder's largest community park. After off-leash playtime, you can walk the Foothills Trail to Wonderland Lake for a more complete outing. The Flatirons provide a stunning backdrop—your dog won't care, but you'll appreciate it.

Features

  • Large dog area: Main fenced section for all dogs
  • Small dog area: Separate fenced section for dogs under 25 pounds
  • Water stations: Multiple water sources for dogs
  • Shade structures: Covered areas for hot days
  • Double-gate entries: Secure entrances
  • Benches: Seating throughout
  • Waste stations: Bags and trash cans provided

Tips

Weekend mornings: Busiest time—great for social dogs who want playmates, potentially overwhelming for shy dogs.

Combine with trails: The Foothills Trail connects right from the park. Take a leashed walk after dog park time.

Parking: Large free lot at Foothills Community Park. Rarely fills except during events.

Rules

  • Dogs must have current vaccinations
  • Aggressive dogs must leave
  • Owners must supervise at all times
  • Clean up after your dog
  • No more than 3 dogs per person

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Foothills Dog Park fenced?

Yes, fully fenced with separate areas for large and small dogs.

Is there parking?

Yes, free parking in the large Foothills Community Park lot.

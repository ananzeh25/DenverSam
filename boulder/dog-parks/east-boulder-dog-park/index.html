East Boulder Dog Park: Spacious Off-Leash Area

East Boulder Dog Park at a glance: Boulder's largest fenced off-leash area with wide-open fields and Flatirons views. Separate areas for large and small dogs. Water access, shade shelters, and plenty of room to run. Located at 2401 Teller Court. Free entry with free parking.

Overview

Location: 2401 Teller Court, Boulder
Size: ~6 acres
Hours: 5am-11pm
Entry Fee: Free

East Boulder Dog Park is the go-to spot for dogs who need space to run. The large open fields provide room for fetch, chasing, and general zooming that smaller parks can't offer. Mountain views add to the experience. It's especially popular with owners of high-energy breeds.

Features

  • Large dog area: Several acres of open, fenced space for dogs of all sizes
  • Small dog area: Separate fenced section for dogs under 25 pounds
  • Water features: Water access for dogs to cool off and play
  • Shade structures: Shelters provide relief from summer sun
  • Double-gate entries: Secure entrances prevent escape
  • Benches: Seating throughout for owners
  • Waste stations: Bags and receptacles provided

Tips

Best times: Early morning and evening avoid summer heat. Weekend mornings bring the biggest crowds (and most playmates).

Water: Bring your own water bowl and fresh water, especially in summer. The water features are for play, not drinking.

Recall: The large open space means dogs can get far away. Make sure your dog has reliable recall before unleashing.

Facilities

  • Free parking lot
  • Portable restrooms
  • Drinking fountain for humans
  • Waste bag dispensers

Rules

  • Dogs must have current vaccinations
  • Aggressive dogs must leave immediately
  • Owners must supervise at all times
  • Clean up after your dog
  • No more than 3 dogs per owner
  • Children under 8 should be supervised closely

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to use the dog park?

No, East Boulder Dog Park is free and open to all Boulder-area residents and visitors.

Is there a separate area for small dogs?

Yes, there's a dedicated fenced area for dogs under 25 pounds.

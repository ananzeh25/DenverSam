Valmont Dog Park: Mountain View Off-Leash Area

Valmont Dog Park at a glance: A well-designed off-leash area within Valmont City Park. Separate sections for large and small dogs, good drainage after rain, and Flatirons views. Adjacent to the famous Valmont Bike Park and disc golf course. East Boulder location with ample parking.

Overview

Location: Valmont City Park, 3371 Valmont Rd, Boulder
Hours: 5am-11pm
Entry Fee: Free

Valmont Dog Park combines good design with good views. The surface drains well, so it's usable soon after rain (unlike some parks that turn into mud pits). The location within the larger Valmont City Park complex means you can combine dog time with disc golf, watch mountain bikers at the famous bike park, or enjoy the open space.

Features

  • Large dog area: Main fenced section with plenty of running room
  • Small dog area: Separate fenced space for dogs under 25 pounds
  • Good drainage: Usable relatively soon after rain
  • Water stations: Dog water fountains
  • Shade shelters: Covered areas for hot days
  • Double-gate entries: Secure entrances prevent escape
  • Benches: Seating throughout
  • Mountain views: Flatirons visible to the west

Location Benefits

Valmont City Park is a recreation hub with the dog park just one attraction:

  • Valmont Bike Park: Famous mountain bike skills park
  • Disc golf course: 18-hole course
  • Sports fields: Soccer and baseball
  • Playground: For families combining activities

Tips

After rain: Valmont drains better than some other Boulder dog parks. Check it out even a day or two after storms.

Combine activities: Bring a disc golf disc, watch the bike park, or use the walking paths while your dog socializes.

Parking: Large free lot. Rarely fills except during major events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a small dog area?

Yes, Valmont has a separate fenced section for dogs under 25 pounds.

Does the park get muddy after rain?

Less than other Boulder dog parks. The surface is designed for good drainage.

Is it close to the bike park?

Yes, they're in the same complex. You can watch the bikers from near the dog park.

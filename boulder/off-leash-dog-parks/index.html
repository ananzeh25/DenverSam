Boulder Dog Parks

Where Boulder dogs run free

Boulder is one of the most dog-friendly cities in America, with multiple dedicated off-leash areas and Voice & Sight tag access to much of the Open Space system. Here are the best places for your pup to run free.

Valmont Dog Park

Boulder's Largest Dog Park

5+ acres of fenced off-leash area with separate small dog section. Shade, water, and plenty of room to run.

Large Area Small Dog Area

East Boulder Dog Park

Prairie Views & Space

Wide open spaces on the east side of town. Less crowded than Valmont with expansive running room.

Spacious Less Crowded

Howard Heuston Dog Park

North Boulder Neighborhood

Neighborhood dog park in north Boulder. Smaller but well-maintained with friendly regular crowd.

Neighborhood Community

Foothills Dog Park

South Boulder Option

Convenient south Boulder location near the community center. Good mix of regulars and visitors.

South Boulder Convenient

Voice & Sight Tag Access

Beyond fenced dog parks, Boulder's Open Space and Mountain Parks system allows off-leash dogs with a Voice & Sight tag on many trails. Dogs must be under voice and sight control at all times. Tags are available from Boulder OSMP for a fee.

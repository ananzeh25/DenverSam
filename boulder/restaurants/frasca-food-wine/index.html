Frasca Food and Wine

Boulder's Crown Jewel

Frasca is Boulder's most celebrated restaurant and one of the finest Italian restaurants in America. Focused on the cuisine of Friuli-Venezia Giulia, it pairs exquisite food with a wine program that has earned every major award. This is Boulder's definitive special occasion destination.

The Food

Chef Lachlan Mackinnon-Patterson focuses exclusively on Friuli-Venezia Giulia, the northeastern Italian region bordering Slovenia and Austria. The menu features handmade pastas, refined proteins, and flavors that are subtler and more Central European than typical Italian fare.

The Wine

Master Sommelier Bobby Stuckey's wine program is legendary. The list emphasizes Friulian producers but spans Italy and beyond. Trust the pairing recommendations‚Äîthe staff knows every bottle intimately.

The Experience

The dining room is elegant but not stuffy. Service is impeccable without being fussy. Plan for a leisurely two-plus hour dinner. This is a meal to savor, not rush.

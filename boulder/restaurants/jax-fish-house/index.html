Jax Fish House

Rocky Mountain Oyster Bar

Jax proves you can get excellent seafood 1,000 miles from any ocean. Fresh oysters flown in daily, a raw bar that rivals coastal spots, and a happy hour that packs the house. It's been a Boulder favorite since 1994.

The Raw Bar

Oysters from both coasts, changed daily based on what's freshest. The shuckers know their stuff and can guide you through the selection. Start with a dozen and work your way through the crudo and ceviche options.

The Kitchen

Beyond the raw bar, Jax serves excellent cooked seafood. The fish of the day is always well-executed, and the classics (lobster rolls, fish tacos, cioppino) are reliable crowd-pleasers.

Happy Hour

The happy hour (4-6pm daily) is legendary. Dollar oysters, discounted drinks, and a lively bar scene. Arrive early‚Äîit gets crowded fast.

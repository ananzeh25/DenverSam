Vail Lakes

Alpine gems in the Gore Range

The mountains surrounding Vail are dotted with stunning alpine lakes, many accessible via scenic hikes through the White River National Forest. From easy access lakes to remote alpine tarns, here are Vail's best lake destinations.

Piney River Ranch

Scenic Lake Access

Beautiful lake with canoe rentals, fishing, and stunning Gore Range views. Accessible by road.

Canoeing Fishing

Booth Lake

Popular Alpine Hike

9-mile roundtrip to a stunning alpine lake with waterfalls along the way. One of Vail's classic hikes.

9 mi RT Moderate

Gore Lake

Gore Range Wilderness

Challenging 12-mile roundtrip to a pristine lake beneath dramatic peaks. Remote wilderness experience.

12 mi RT Hard

Upper Piney Lake

Backcountry Beauty

Hike beyond Piney River Ranch to this remote alpine lake. Less crowded with stunning mountain scenery.

6 mi RT Moderate

Nottingham Lake

Avon Town Lake

Easy access lake in nearby Avon with kayaking, fishing, and a paved walking path. Great for families.

Easy Family

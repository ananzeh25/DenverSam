Vail Hiking Trails

Summer adventures on ski slopes

When the snow melts, Vail transforms into a hiking paradise. From easy nature walks to challenging alpine adventures, the trails around Vail offer stunning scenery and access to pristine wilderness.

Booth Falls Trail

Vail's Most Popular Hike

4.3-mile roundtrip to a stunning 60-foot waterfall. Continue to Booth Lake for a 9-mile adventure.

4.3 mi RT Moderate

Gore Creek Trail

River Walk

Easy paved trail along Gore Creek through Vail Village. Perfect for a morning walk or evening stroll.

Various Easy

Bighorn Creek Trail

Wildflower Paradise

8-mile roundtrip through aspen groves and alpine meadows. Excellent wildflower displays in July.

8 mi RT Moderate

Eagles Nest Wilderness

Backcountry Adventure

Pristine wilderness with multiple trailheads. Alpine lakes, 13ers, and remote backcountry camping.

Various Moderate-Hard

Vail Mountain Summer Trails

Paved Mountain Path

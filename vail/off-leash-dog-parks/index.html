Vail Dog Parks

Mountain adventures with your pup

Vail is a dog-friendly destination with both dedicated dog parks and miles of trails where well-behaved dogs can enjoy the mountain scenery. Here's where to take your four-legged friend.

Stephens Park Dog Park

Vail's Main Dog Park

Fenced off-leash area near East Vail with mountain views. The primary dog park in the Vail Valley.

Fenced Mountain Views

White River National Forest

Off-Leash Trails

Most National Forest trails allow dogs off-leash under voice control. Endless mountain adventures await.

Trails Voice Control

Gore Creek Trail

On-Leash Path

Dogs welcome on-leash along Gore Creek through Vail Village. Easy walking with river access.

On-Leash River

Nottingham Lake (Avon)

Lakeside Walk

Dog-friendly paved path around Nottingham Lake in nearby Avon. Easy walking with lake views.

Vail Attractions

World-class mountain resort

Vail is Colorado's premier ski resort, combining European-inspired alpine village charm with North America's largest ski terrain. From winter skiing to summer festivals, Vail delivers year-round mountain luxury.

Vail Mountain Resort

North America's Largest Ski Resort

5,317 acres of skiing across front-side bowls and legendary Back Bowls. World-class terrain for all abilities.

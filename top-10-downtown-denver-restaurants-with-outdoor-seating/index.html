Top 10 Downtown Denver Restaurants with Outdoor Seating

Denver's vibrant downtown boasts a diverse culinary scene, with many restaurants offering delightful outdoor seating.

Here are some popular dining options, highlighting some frequently enjoyed dishes and drinks:

Culinary Dropout

Culinary Dropout serves modern American comfort food. Popular dishes include Pretzel Bites with Provolone Fondue and 36-Hour Pork Ribs.

The menu also features salads, sandwiches, pizzas, and other entrees, along with a cocktail menu.

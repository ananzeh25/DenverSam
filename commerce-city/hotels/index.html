Commerce City Hotels

Quick Info

Location: Northeast Denver metro. Nearby: DIA, Dick's Sporting Goods Park, Rocky Mountain Arsenal. Access: I-70, I-76, E-470. Price range: Budget to mid-range. Best for: Airport access, Rapids games, Arsenal visits.

Commerce City hotels serve visitors to Denver International Airport, Dick's Sporting Goods Park, and Rocky Mountain Arsenal. The location offers convenient highway access with typically lower rates than downtown Denver.

Location Benefits

Near DIA

Closer to the airport than downtown Denver. Good for early flights or late arrivals.

Stadium Access

Near Dick's Sporting Goods Park for Rapids games, concerts, and events.

Wildlife Refuge

Close to Rocky Mountain Arsenal for early morning wildlife viewing.

Hotel Options

Chain Hotels

Major chain properties along I-70 and E-470 corridors. Consistent amenities.

Budget Options

Affordable options for cost-conscious travelers.

Extended Stay

Properties with kitchens for longer visits.

Common Amenities

Free Parking

Most hotels offer free parking—useful for airport travelers.

Breakfast

Many properties include complimentary breakfast.

Airport Shuttles

Some hotels offer DIA shuttle service.

Tips

Book for game days. Hotels fill for major events at Dick's Sporting Goods Park.

Check airport shuttles. Convenient for DIA access.

Early Arsenal visits. Stay nearby for sunrise wildlife viewing.

Compare to DIA hotels. Commerce City may be cheaper than airport hotels.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far to Denver airport?

15-25 minutes depending on location and traffic.

How far to downtown Denver?

About 15-20 minutes via I-70 or I-76.

Are there hotels near the stadium?

Limited options immediately adjacent. Most hotels along highway corridors.

Is Commerce City safe?

Areas near hotels and attractions are generally fine. Standard city precautions apply.

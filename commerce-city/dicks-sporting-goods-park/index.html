Dick's Sporting Goods Park

Quick Info

Type: Soccer stadium and sports complex. Capacity: 18,000+. Team: Colorado Rapids (MLS). Events: Soccer, concerts, lacrosse. Complex: 24 additional soccer fields. Best for: Soccer fans, concerts, youth tournaments.

Dick's Sporting Goods Park is the home of the Colorado Rapids—Major League Soccer's Colorado franchise. Beyond professional soccer, the massive complex hosts concerts, youth tournaments, and other sporting events.

Colorado Rapids

MLS Soccer

The Rapids play MLS home games at the stadium from spring through fall. The team won the MLS Cup in 2010.

Game Day Experience

Pregame tailgating in the parking lots. Supporters' groups bring energy with chants and displays.

Tickets

Tickets available through the Rapids website or resale sites. Various seating options from general admission to premium.

Other Events

Concerts

Major concert venue for outdoor summer shows. National touring acts perform on the stadium stage.

Lacrosse

Professional and collegiate lacrosse events periodically hosted at the stadium.

Rugby

Rugby matches and tournaments use the stadium and surrounding fields.

Sports Complex

Additional Fields

24 full-size soccer fields surround the stadium. Used for youth tournaments, adult leagues, and training.

Tournaments

Major youth soccer tournaments bring teams from across the country.

Tips

Arrive early. Parking lots open well before events for tailgating.

Bring sunscreen. Open-air stadium with lots of sun exposure.

Check traffic. Events can create traffic on E-470 and surrounding roads.

Carpool. Large parking lots but can fill for popular events.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get tickets to Rapids games?

Through the Colorado Rapids website, Ticketmaster, or resale sites.

Is there parking?

Large parking lots on-site. Fees apply for events.

Can I tailgate?

Yes—tailgating is popular in the parking lots before Rapids games.

Are there concerts?

Yes—major outdoor concerts during summer months.

Reunion

Quick Info

Type: Master-planned community. Features: Parks, trails, rec centers, pools. Style: New construction, family-oriented. Location: Northeast Commerce City near DIA. Amenities: Community pools, clubhouses, events. Best for: Families, new homes, community living.

Reunion is one of Colorado's largest master-planned communities—a growing neighborhood in Commerce City featuring extensive parks, trails, recreation centers, and family amenities. The community has become a hub for new home construction in the north metro.

Community Amenities

Recreation Centers

Community rec centers with fitness facilities, pools, and activity spaces for residents.

Pools

Multiple community pools throughout Reunion, including water features for summer fun.

Parks

Numerous parks with playgrounds, sports fields, and open space.

Trails

Connected trail system linking neighborhoods, parks, and community areas. Great for walking, biking, and running.

Location

Near DIA

Convenient location for airport workers and frequent travelers. Quick access to DIA.

Highway Access

E-470 and I-76 provide regional connections. Easy access to downtown Denver and surrounding areas.

Community Life

Events

Community events throughout the year including holiday celebrations, summer activities, and neighborhood gatherings.

Schools

Schools within the community serve Reunion families.

Shopping

Growing retail and dining options as the community expands.

Tips

Visit the rec center. Best way to see community amenities.

Explore the trails. Well-maintained paths connect the community.

Check events calendar. Regular community activities and events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Reunion a nice area?

Yes—newer development with extensive amenities and well-maintained common areas.

How far to Denver?

About 20 minutes to downtown Denver. Near DIA for airport access.

Are there HOA fees?

Yes—HOA fees fund community amenities and maintenance.

Is it still growing?

Yes—new construction continues with additional phases planned.

Getting to Commerce City

Quick Info

From Denver: 10-15 min via I-70 or I-76. From DIA: 15-20 min via E-470 or Peña. Key highways: I-70, I-76, E-470. Transit: Limited RTD bus service. Car recommended: Yes.

Commerce City sits northeast of Denver, easily accessible via I-70, I-76, and E-470. The area is well-positioned between downtown Denver and the airport, though a car is recommended for getting around.

From Downtown Denver

Via I-70

I-70 east to Quebec Street, Colorado Boulevard, or other Commerce City exits. About 10-15 minutes.

Via I-76

I-25 north to I-76 east. Exit at 88th Ave or other Commerce City exits. About 15 minutes.

Surface Streets

Quebec Street or Colorado Boulevard north from Denver. Slower but avoids highways.

From Denver Airport (DIA)

Via E-470

E-470 south from the airport, then west on 56th Ave or other exits. About 15-20 minutes. Tolls apply.

Via Peña Boulevard

Peña Boulevard to I-70, then west to Commerce City exits. May avoid some tolls.

To Dick's Sporting Goods Park

From Denver

I-70 east to Quebec Street, north to 60th Ave. Or E-470 to 56th Ave. Follow event signage.

Event Parking

Stadium parking lots open for events. Expect traffic before and after major events.

To Rocky Mountain Arsenal

Main Entrance

Quebec Street entrance on the west side. Havana Street entrance on the east side.

Tips

Car recommended. Limited public transit. Most destinations require driving.

E-470 tolls. Convenient but tolls add up. Plan alternative routes if cost is a concern.

Event traffic. Allow extra time around Dick's Sporting Goods Park for games and concerts.

GPS recommended. Commerce City sprawls. Use navigation for specific destinations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there public transit to Commerce City?

Limited RTD bus service. A car is recommended.

How far is Commerce City from Denver?

About 10 miles northeast of downtown. 10-20 minutes by car.

Is E-470 worth the toll?

For airport trips, usually yes. Faster and less traffic than surface routes.

Is there Uber/Lyft?

Yes—rideshare services operate throughout Commerce City.

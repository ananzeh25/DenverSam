Centennial Hotels

Quick Info

Location: South Denver metro. Nearby: Denver Tech Center, Park Meadows. Types: Business hotels, extended stay, budget options. Access: I-25, light rail, 25 min to DIA. Best for: Business travelers, families visiting south metro.

Centennial and the adjacent Denver Tech Center area offer numerous hotel options, primarily serving business travelers. The location provides easy access to south metro corporate offices while offering light rail connections to downtown Denver.

Hotel Areas

Denver Tech Center

The DTC area along I-25 has the highest concentration of hotels. Full-service business hotels, select-service properties, and extended-stay options.

Near Park Meadows

Hotels near Park Meadows Mall offer shopping and dining within walking distance.

Arapahoe Road Corridor

Additional hotels along Arapahoe Road near I-25. Various price points.

Hotel Types

Full-Service Business Hotels

Marriott, Hyatt, Hilton properties in the DTC with restaurants, meeting rooms, and full amenities.

Select-Service

Courtyard, Hampton Inn, and similar brands offering reliable comfort with fewer amenities.

Extended Stay

Residence Inn, Homewood Suites, and extended-stay properties for longer visits. Kitchen facilities included.

Budget Options

La Quinta, Holiday Inn Express, and budget chains for cost-conscious travelers.

Common Amenities

Free Parking

Most suburban hotels include free parking—significant savings compared to downtown Denver.

Breakfast

Many properties include breakfast. Check when booking.

Fitness & Pools

Indoor pools and fitness centers common at mid-range and above properties.

Why Stay in Centennial

Business Access

Close to Tech Center offices and corporate campuses. Convenient for business travelers.

Value

Often lower rates than downtown Denver with more space and free parking.

Light Rail

E and H lines connect to downtown Denver. About 30-40 minutes to Union Station.

Tips

Weekday rates. Business hotels may be cheaper on weekends when corporate travel drops.

Light rail access. Consider hotels near stations for downtown visits without driving.

Park Meadows proximity. Hotels near the mall offer convenient dining and shopping.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Centennial from downtown Denver?

About 15 miles via I-25, or 30-40 minutes by light rail.

How far from DIA?

About 25-30 miles, depending on exact location. Allow 30-45 minutes.

Is it a good base for tourists?

Works if you have a car and want suburban convenience. Downtown is better for sightseeing-focused visits.

What's the Tech Center?

Denver Tech Center (DTC) is a major business district with corporate offices, spanning Centennial and neighboring cities.

Is there good dining nearby?

Yes—Park Meadows, Streets at SouthGlenn, and DTC area have numerous restaurants.

Golf Near Centennial

Quick Info

Nearby courses: Lone Tree Golf Club, South Suburban courses. Types: Public and semi-private. Season: Year-round, weather permitting. Booking: Online tee times. Rates: Affordable public to upscale options.

Centennial and the surrounding south metro area offer several golf options, from affordable municipal courses to well-maintained public facilities. South Suburban Parks and Recreation operates multiple courses in the area.

Nearby Courses

Lone Tree Golf Club

Public course in adjacent Lone Tree. Championship layout with good conditions. Clubhouse and practice facilities. One of the nicer public options in the south metro.

South Suburban Courses

South Suburban Parks and Recreation operates several courses including Littleton Golf and Tennis Club. Affordable rates for district residents.

Family Golf

Par-3 and executive courses in the area for beginners and quick rounds.

Course Features

Conditions

South metro courses generally well-maintained. Peak season (May-September) offers best conditions.

Terrain

Mostly flat to gently rolling terrain typical of suburban Denver courses.

Practice Facilities

Driving ranges and practice greens at most full-size courses.

Booking & Rates

Tee Times

Book online through course websites or GolfNow. Advance booking recommended for weekend mornings.

Rates

Range from affordable municipal rates to higher-end public course pricing. Resident discounts at South Suburban courses.

Twilight Deals

Afternoon and twilight rates offer savings. Good for summer evening rounds.

Tips

Book ahead. Weekend mornings fill up. Reserve online in advance.

Check district rates. South Suburban residents get discounted rates at district courses.

Altitude plays. Ball travels farther at altitude—club down on approaches.

Spring and fall. Shoulder seasons can offer great conditions with fewer crowds.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best public course nearby?

Lone Tree Golf Club is well-regarded. South Suburban courses offer good value.

Can I walk?

Most courses allow walking, though carts are common. Walking rates typically lower.

Are there lessons available?

Yes—most courses offer instruction through their pro shops.

How much does a round cost?

Varies by course and time. Public courses range from $30-70 for 18 holes.

Can I rent clubs?

Yes—rental clubs available at most courses.

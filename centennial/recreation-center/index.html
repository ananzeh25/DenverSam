South Suburban Recreation

Quick Info

District: South Suburban Parks and Recreation. Facilities: Multiple rec centers with pools, fitness, sports. Programs: Classes, leagues, camps. Access: Day passes and memberships. Service area: Centennial, Littleton, and surrounding areas.

Centennial is served by South Suburban Parks and Recreation District, which operates multiple recreation centers and facilities across the south metro. These centers offer pools, fitness equipment, sports courts, and extensive programming.

Nearby Facilities

Goodson Recreation Center

Located in Centennial, Goodson offers pools, fitness center, gymnasium, and programs. One of the closest facilities for Centennial residents.

Buck Recreation Center

In Littleton with pools including an outdoor water park, fitness, and sports facilities.

Family Sports Center

Dome facility for indoor sports including soccer, lacrosse, and more.

Common Amenities

Pools

Indoor and outdoor pools at various facilities. Lap swimming, water fitness, lessons, and family play.

Fitness Centers

Cardio equipment, weight machines, and free weights. Well-maintained facilities.

Group Fitness

Classes including yoga, spin, strength training, and aerobics. Schedules vary by location.

Courts & Gyms

Basketball, volleyball, racquetball, and other court sports. Open gym and organized play.

Programs

Youth Programs

Swim lessons, youth sports leagues, day camps, and after-school programs throughout the year.

Adult Programs

Sports leagues, fitness classes, and specialized programs for adults.

Senior Programs

Fitness and social programs designed for seniors.

Access & Fees

Day Passes

Day passes available for visitors. Access to pools and fitness facilities.

Memberships

Monthly and annual options. District resident and non-resident rates.

Tips

Check district boundaries. Resident rates apply to those within the South Suburban district.

Register early. Popular programs and leagues fill quickly.

Check schedules. Pool and facility hours vary. Verify online before visiting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can non-residents use the facilities?

Yes—day passes and non-resident memberships available at higher rates.

Which facility is best for families?

Buck Recreation Center has an outdoor water park popular with families in summer. Goodson is convenient for Centennial.

Is there childcare?

Some facilities offer drop-in childcare. Check individual locations for availability.

How much is a day pass?

Varies by facility and resident status. Check South Suburban website for current rates.

Can I just swim?

Yes—day passes give access to pools. Check lap swim and open swim schedules.

Centennial Center Park

Quick Info

Size: 11 acres. Features: Amphitheater, splash pad, playground, trails. Events: Summer concerts, movies, community gatherings. Location: 13050 E Peakview Ave. Hours: Dawn to dusk. Fee: Free.

Centennial Center Park is the city's signature gathering space—an 11-acre park built as Centennial's civic center. The park hosts community events, summer concerts, and serves as the heart of city activities.

Park Features

Amphitheater

Outdoor amphitheater hosts summer concert series, movies in the park, and community events. Bring blankets and chairs for lawn seating.

Splash Pad

Interactive water feature for kids. Open during summer months—great for cooling off on hot days.

Playground

Modern playground equipment for various ages. Well-maintained and popular with families.

Walking Trails

Paved paths wind through the park. Connect to the broader trail system for longer walks.

Events

Summer Concert Series

Free outdoor concerts throughout summer. Live music across various genres. Arrive early for good spots.

Movies in the Park

Outdoor movie screenings during summer evenings. Family-friendly films on the big screen.

Community Events

Holiday celebrations, festivals, and city events throughout the year. Check Centennial's event calendar.

Amenities

Picnic Areas

Tables and lawn space for picnics. Bring your own food or grab something nearby.

Restrooms

Restroom facilities available. May have seasonal hours.

Parking

Free parking lot. Can fill up during major events—arrive early.

Tips

Concert tips. Bring blankets, chairs, and snacks. Arrive early for summer concerts.

Splash pad timing. Best on hot afternoons. Check seasonal hours.

Event parking. Consider carpooling or arriving early for popular events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the park free?

Yes—free admission. Events are also typically free to attend.

When is the splash pad open?

Typically Memorial Day through Labor Day, weather permitting. Check city website for hours.

Can I reserve the park for events?

Contact the City of Centennial for information on reservations and permits.

Are dogs allowed?

Yes, on leash. Clean up after your pet.

Is there food at concerts?

Food trucks often present at major events. Bringing your own food is also permitted.
